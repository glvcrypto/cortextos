## Why Winterize

A Sault Ste. Marie winter regularly drops below −20 °C. Water that sits in an engine block, a lower unit or a cooling passage expands when it freezes, and metal cracks. Fuel that sits in a tank for five months goes off and gums up the carburetor or injectors.

What you're paying to avoid:

- **Engine block cracks** from trapped water freezing inside the powerhead
- **Lower unit failure** from water sitting in the gear case over winter
- **Fuel system damage** from old ethanol-blend gas turning into varnish
- **Battery damage** from a discharged battery freezing solid
- **Hull and upholstery damage** from condensation, mice and ice load

A proper winterization runs a few hundred dollars. A cracked engine block on a four-stroke outboard runs into the thousands. The math is straightforward.

## What's Included in Reyco's Winterization Service

Our standard boat winterization covers everything the Mercury service procedure calls for, plus the practical extras a Northern Ontario winter actually demands:

If your boat is on a trailer, you can drop it here. If it's in storage somewhere else, mention that when you call and we'll work out the logistics.

## Boat Winterization — Common Questions

**When should I winterize my boat in Sault Ste. Marie?**
Mid-August to mid-September is ideal. By late September the bench is filling fast and you're racing the first hard frost. If you wait until October, book at least two weeks ahead. After the first freeze, damage may already be done before we touch the boat.

**Can I winterize my boat myself?**
Some boaters do, and if you have the manual, the parts and the time, it can be done in a driveway. The risk is missing a step — water trapped in a single cooling passage will crack a powerhead, and a fuel line that wasn't fogged properly will gum up by spring. If you DIY and miss something, the bill in spring is bigger than the winterization service. Our package follows the Mercury procedure exactly and includes an inspection report.

**What's the difference between winterization and just storing the boat?**
Storage is what you do with the boat after winterization is done. Winterization is the engine, fuel system and cooling work that keeps the boat from freeze damage. Both matter — but winterization is the one that protects the expensive parts.

**Do you winterize boats you didn't sell?**
Yes. We winterize most makes and engine brands. Mercury and Princecraft are our authorised lines, but if you have a different outboard, sterndrive or hull, call us with the make and model and we'll quote it.

**Can I store my boat at Reyco over winter?**
Call us about storage availability. Indoor storage is limited and books up fast — outdoor with shrink-wrap is the usual option, and we can quote both with your winterization package.

### Spelling note for review

1. **Search keyword is "winterization"** — the primary keyword is `boat winterization sault ste marie`. Canadian boaters search the -ize form 4-5x more than -ise on this term.

3. **Mercury's own service procedure docs** call it "winterization," so the brand-canonical exception (per MEMORY.md line 11) applies.
