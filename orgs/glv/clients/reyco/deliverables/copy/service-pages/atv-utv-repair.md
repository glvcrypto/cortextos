## ATV Repair Services

Whether you ride your quad on a trapline, a job site or the trails behind the camp, the parts that wear are pretty consistent. Our shop handles:

- Engine starting and tuning issues (carb work, spark, compression)
- Drive train, CVT and clutch service
- Suspension, bearings and steering
- Electrical (charging system, starter, lights, gauges)
- Brake service and pad replacement
- Tire mount and balance
- Annual scheduled maintenance per the Hisun service interval
- Pre-season check-up

If you bought your ATV from us, we already have the service history. If you didn't, bring it in with the make and model and we'll work from there.

## UTV / Side-by-Side Repair Services

Side-by-sides take more abuse than most owners admit. Cab work, hauling load, water, dust, and the kind of long days a quad would never see. Our UTV bench handles:

- Engine, drive train, clutch and CVT service
- Cab, roof and accessory installation (winches, plows, racks, lights)
- Hydraulics and tilt-bed service
- Suspension and shock service
- Brake and tire service
- Electrical and accessory wiring
- Annual scheduled maintenance per the Hisun side-by-side service interval

We use "side-by-side" and "UTV" interchangeably here — most owners use both, depending on how they grew up calling them.

## How service works at Reyco

Drop the unit off, or call ahead and we'll book a slot on Lee's bench. Here's the usual flow:

1. **Intake.** We tag the unit, write down what's wrong (or what you've noticed), and get your contact info.
2. **Diagnostic.** Lee inspects the engine, drive train, electrical and the parts most likely to be at fault for what you described.
3. **Estimate first, then approval.** We call you with a clear price before any work starts. No surprise bills.
4. **Repair.** Most jobs use OEM Hisun parts pulled from our parts room. Same-day pickup if it's small, 3 to 5 business days if it's bigger or we're waiting on a part.
5. **Pickup.** We test it before you take it home. If it's not running right, we'd rather catch that on our bench than 30 km out on the trail.

## Common ATV and Side-by-Side Issues We Fix

A few things we see week to week:

- Won't start after sitting all winter (almost always fuel-related — old gas, gummed carburetor, weak battery)
- Loses power up hill or under load
- CVT belt slipping or squealing
- Drive train clunks or shifts hard
- Electrical gremlins (no spark, charge issues, gauge drop-outs)
- Suspension sag or loose steering
- Brakes spongy or pulling

If you're not sure what's going on, describe it when you call. Lee can usually tell from the symptoms whether it's an hour on the bench or a full diagnostic.

## ATV & Side-by-Side Repair — Common Questions

**How long does ATV or side-by-side repair take?**
Most repairs take 3 to 5 business days. Simple jobs like an annual service or a CVT belt swap can be same day if the bench isn't backed up. We'll give you a realistic estimate when you drop the unit off.

**How much does ATV or side-by-side repair cost?**
A typical annual service runs 1 to 2 hours of bench time plus parts. Carburetor work is 1 to 3 hours plus parts. Major engine or drive train repairs get a written quote after the diagnostic. Either way, we call you with a clear number before any work starts.

**Do you service brands beyond Hisun?**
Yes. We're Hisun's authorised dealer in Sault Ste. Marie, but our techs work on most major ATV and side-by-side brands. If you're not sure whether we cover yours, call ahead with the make and model and we'll tell you straight.

**My ATV won't start after sitting all winter — what's the most common cause?**
Old fuel. Modern ethanol-blend gas goes off in three to six months and gums up the carburetor. Drain the tank, run fresh fuel through, and if it still won't fire we can take it from there. A quick carburetor clean often gets you back on the trail without a full rebuild.

**What should be checked at each ATV or side-by-side service interval?**
At minimum: engine oil and filter, air filter, spark plug, CVT belt condition, brake pads and fluid, drive train oil, coolant level, tire condition and pressure, and a full electrical check. We follow the Hisun service interval for warranty-covered units; for older units we use the same intervals as a sensible baseline.

**Can I order parts for my ATV or side-by-side through Reyco?**
Yes. We stock OEM Hisun parts and can order in OEM parts for most other major brands. See our [parts page](/service/order-parts/) or call us with your make, model and serial number and we'll check stock or order it in.
