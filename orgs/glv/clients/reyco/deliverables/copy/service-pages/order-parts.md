## Find Your Model Number

You can't order the right part without the right model and serial number. Here's where to look on common equipment:

- **Outboard motor.** Mercury serial tag is on the transom bracket (the silver plate where the engine clamps to the boat). Look for "MOD" and "SER" lines.
- **Lawn mower (push or self-propelled).** Tag is usually on the deck near the rear, or under the seat on a riding mower. Cub Cadet and Toro both use a 11-character model number.
- **Riding mower or zero-turn.** Tag is under the seat or on the steering tower.
- **Snowblower.** Tag is on the back of the auger housing, or behind the engine.
- **Chainsaw or trimmer.** Tag is on the engine housing, often near the recoil starter.
- **ATV or side-by-side.** VIN is on the frame near the steering or under the seat. Hisun also lists a model number on the engine block.
- **Trolling motor (Minn Kota).** Tag is on the motor housing at the top of the shaft.

If you can't find the tag, snap a photo of the equipment and email it to **parts@reycomarine.com**. We can usually identify the model from the photo and pull the serial later.

## Parts — Common Questions

**Can I order parts online from Reyco?**
We're rolling out a live online inventory browser through Lightspeed in 2026 — call ahead in the meantime. Phone (705-253-7828) and email (parts@reycomarine.com) are the fastest ways to confirm stock and price right now.

**OEM or aftermarket — does it really matter?**
Yes, especially for warranty work. OEM parts are made by (or for) the manufacturer specifically for your model. Aftermarket parts are third-party and may fit, may not, and can void your warranty if installed during the warranty period. For service we do here, we use OEM. If you want aftermarket for a non-warranty job, we'll order it where we can, but we'll flag the warranty implication first.

**How long does a special-order part take?**
Most OEM orders from Mercury, Cub Cadet, Toro and Echo land within a week. Hisun, Princecraft and some lower-volume parts can take two to three weeks depending on factory stock. We'll quote a real lead time before you commit — no "should be in soon."

**What's your return policy on parts?**
Unused, unopened OEM parts in original packaging are returnable within a reasonable window (call to confirm details for your order). Special-order parts and electrical components are typically non-returnable once shipped from the manufacturer — we'll flag that at the time of order.

**How do I find my model number?**
See the section above. If you can't find the tag, email a photo of the equipment to **parts@reycomarine.com** and we can usually identify the model from the photo.

**Do you ship parts outside Sault Ste. Marie?**
Yes — call us with your address and we'll quote shipping. Most marine parts ship by ground through Canada Post or Purolator. ATV and snowblower parts go by ground freight depending on size.
