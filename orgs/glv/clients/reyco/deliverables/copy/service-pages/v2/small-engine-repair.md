### Section A — How Service Works (Process)

Drop the equipment off, or call ahead and we'll book a slot on Damian's bench. Here's the usual flow:

1. **Drop-off and intake.** We tag the unit, write down what's wrong (or what you've noticed), and get your contact info.
2. **Diagnostic.** Damian inspects the engine — compression, fuel, spark, and the parts most likely to be at fault for what you described.
3. **Estimate first, then approval.** We call you with a clear price before any work starts. No surprise bills.
4. **Repair.** Most jobs use OEM parts pulled straight from the parts room. Same-day pickup if it's small, 3 to 5 business days if it's bigger or we're waiting on a part.
5. **Pickup.** We test it before you take it home. If it's not running right, we'd rather catch that on our bench than in your driveway.

### Section B — How Long It Takes (Duration)

Most small engine repairs take **3 to 5 business days**, depending on what's wrong and what's on the bench when your unit lands.

- **Same-day** if it's a quick fix (spark plug swap, simple carburetor clean) and the bench isn't backed up.
- **3 to 5 business days** for typical repairs — diagnostic, parts pull, repair, test, pickup.

If your equipment isn't running and you need it back fast, mention that when you call. We try to fit emergency jobs in around scheduled service when bench capacity allows.

### Section C — What We Fix (Equipment & Issues)

If it has a small engine and it lives outside the house, our shop services it:

- [Lawn mowers](/service/lawn-equipment/) — push, self-propelled, riding, zero-turn
- [Snowblowers](/service/snow-equipment/) — single, two and three-stage
- Chainsaws and string trimmers
- Leaf blowers and pressure washers
- Generators and water pumps
- Most other 2-stroke and 4-stroke gas engines under 30 hp

A few signs your small engine is overdue for service:

- It's been sitting since last season and won't start
- It runs but loses power under load
- Smoke from the muffler (especially blue or black)
- Pull cord is loose, frayed, or won't recoil
- Blade or bar isn't cutting clean
- You've poured fuel stabiliser in and it still struggles

If there's an old can of last year's gas in the tank, that's the most common one we see in the spring. Modern ethanol-blend fuel goes off in three to six months. Empty the tank, run fresh fuel through, and if it still won't fire, we can take it from there.

## 5. FAQ — Common Questions

**How long does small engine repair take?**
Most repairs take 3 to 5 business days. Simple jobs like a spark plug swap or a quick carburetor clean can be same day if the bench isn't backed up. We'll give you a realistic estimate when you drop the unit off.

**How much does small engine repair cost?**
A typical spring tune-up runs 1 to 2 hours of bench time plus parts. Carburetor work is 1 to 3 hours plus parts. Major engine repairs get a written quote after the diagnostic. Either way, we call you with a clear number before any work starts.
