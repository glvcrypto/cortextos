## Our Story

Reyco Marine & Small Engine has been serving Sault Ste. Marie boaters, contractors and homeowners for more than 60 years. Today we run a full-service facility on White Oak Drive — parts room, sales floor, marine bench and small engine bench all under one roof.

What that means in practice: when you bring something in, the techs working on it sit a few steps from the parts they need and the people who sold the unit. No back-and-forth between buildings. No chasing parts from a third-party warehouse. The whole job, start to finish, happens here.

We're an authorised dealer for the brands we sell — Mercury, Princecraft, Minn Kota, Humminbird, Cub Cadet, Toro, Echo, Bercomac and Hisun — which means warranty work and OEM parts are handled in-house, not sent back to head office.

## What We Do

The shop is built around three things:

- **Sales.** New marine packages, outdoor power equipment, ATVs and side-by-sides. We size the gear to how you'll actually use it — the boat that fits your lake, the mower that fits your lot, the ATV that fits your trail.
- **Service and repair.** [Marine](/service/marine/), [lawn equipment](/service/lawn-equipment/), [snow equipment](/service/snow-equipment/), [small engine](/service/small-engine/), [ATV/UTV](/service/atv-utv-repair/) — all on the same benches with the same techs every season. Annual [tune-ups](/service/tune-ups/), [winterization](/service/winterization/), [spring commissioning](/service/spring-commissioning/) and [warranty claims](/service/warranty/) all run through this shop.
- **Parts.** OEM parts for every brand we carry, [stocked in-house](/service/order-parts/). What we don't have, we order in.

## Meet the Team

Reyco runs with a small, steady crew. The same faces, year after year — which is why most regulars know everyone in the shop by name.

**Casey — Owner / Sales**
Casey leads Reyco as owner and the face of the sales floor. He works with customers from first walk-in through final delivery, making sure every package fits how they actually use the gear.

**Aaron — Co-Owner / Sales**
Aaron co-owns Reyco and works the sales floor alongside Casey. He's the one walking customers through boat packages and outdoor power equipment, matching the right unit to the way it's actually going to get used.

**Lynn — Parts Manager**
Lynn manages the parts room and keeps the OEM inventory moving across every brand we carry. If you've ever called or emailed for a part and got a real answer the same day, that's Lynn's system at work.

**Ron — Parts Specialist**
Ron works the parts counter day to day — checking stock, sourcing what's not on the shelf, and getting the right OEM number into the right hands. Walk-ins, phone calls and special orders all run through Ron.

**Kory — Sales**
Kory works the sales floor with Casey and Aaron. Whether you're sizing up a new boat package or matching equipment to a job, Kory walks through the options without the upsell.

**Lee — Service Tech (big engines, boats, ATV/UTV)**
Lee runs our marine and big-engine service bench. Outboards, sterndrives, ATVs and side-by-sides all come through him — from spring commissioning to mid-season repair.

**Damian — Service Tech (small engines, lawn mowers, snow blowers, chainsaws)**
Damian runs our small engine bench. Lawn mowers, snowblowers, chainsaws and trimmers — anything with a small engine that needs a tune-up or repair lands on his table.
