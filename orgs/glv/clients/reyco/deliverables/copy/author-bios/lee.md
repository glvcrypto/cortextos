## Bio

Lee runs the marine and big-engine service bench at Reyco Marine & Small Engine. Outboards, sterndrives, ATVs and side-by-sides — anything with a bigger engine that needs a tune-up, a diagnostic or a full repair lands on Lee's table.

The bench works on a seasonal rhythm. [Spring commissioning](/service/spring-commissioning/) hits hard from March through April — boats coming out of winter storage that need a full water-ready check before launch. The summer is rolling repair work — outboards that won't start, lower units that took a knock, electrical issues that show up on a Saturday morning before the lake. The fall is [winterization](/service/winterization/) — fogging, draining, fluid swaps, the routine that keeps an outboard from cracking a block over a Northern Ontario freeze. ATV and side-by-side work runs year-round.

Reyco is an authorised dealer for [Mercury](/service/marine/) and Princecraft, which means warranty work on outboards and hulls is handled here directly — no shipping units back to the manufacturer, no waiting on pre-approval calls. (See the [warranty page](/service/warranty/) for how that flow works.) Same for Hisun on the ATV side.

Lee works closely with Lynn and Ron on the parts side — the OEM parts the marine bench moves through most often (impellers, plugs, lower unit gear oil, fuel filters, water separators) are kept stocked because of how often Lee pulls them.

To book marine, big-engine or off-road service, call **705-253-7828** or stop by **11 White Oak Drive East**.
