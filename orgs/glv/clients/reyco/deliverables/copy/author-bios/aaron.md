## Bio

Aaron co-owns Reyco Marine & Small Engine and works the sales floor alongside Casey. Between the two of them, almost every package that leaves the shop has had a face-to-face conversation with one or both — about what the gear's going to do, where it's going to live, and what's worth spending a little more on versus what's not.

Aaron's role on the floor leans into the matching-equipment-to-job conversation. Boat packages where the customer hasn't decided yet between a 16-foot tiller and a 17-foot console. Outdoor power equipment where the question is push mower vs self-propelled, or whether a riding mower actually makes sense for the lot. ATV and side-by-side packages where the trail conditions and trailer setup change which model fits. Aaron walks through the trade-offs without leaning on the upsell.

He works closely with the parts team (Lynn and Ron) on order-in items and warranty parts, and with Lee and Damian on the service side when a sale ties into a delivery prep or a trade-in service. The shop runs as a small team — sales, parts and service all on one floor — and Aaron's the connective tissue between sales conversations and what the rest of the bench needs to know.

When you call about a major purchase, Aaron is the second person (after Casey) you're most likely to deal with.
