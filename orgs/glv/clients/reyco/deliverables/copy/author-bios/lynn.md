## Bio

Lynn manages the parts room at Reyco Marine & Small Engine. If you've ever called the parts counter and got a real answer about stock the same day — instead of a "let me get back to you in two days" — that's Lynn's system at work behind the scenes.

The job is wider than it sounds. Reyco is an authorised dealer for nine brands across marine, outdoor power and ATV (Mercury, Princecraft, Minn Kota, Humminbird, Cub Cadet, Toro, Echo, Bercomac, Hisun), each with its own catalogue, its own ordering portal, and its own warranty parts process. Lynn keeps the OEM inventory moving across all of them — the parts that turn over weekly stay on the shelf, the slow-movers get ordered in on a real lead time, and the warranty parts get pulled and logged against the right customer file.

She also runs the parts side of the warranty workflow. When a unit comes in for a covered claim, Lynn's the one making sure the right OEM part gets pulled, the manufacturer's paperwork gets filed, and the customer doesn't end up paying out of pocket for something the warranty should have covered. (See the [warranty page](/service/warranty/) for how that flow works end to end.)

Lynn works closely with Ron on the parts counter day to day, and with Lee and Damian on the service bench — the parts they need most often are the ones she keeps stocked.

For parts inquiries, email **parts@reycomarine.com** or call **705-253-7828**. Lynn or Ron will be on the other end.
