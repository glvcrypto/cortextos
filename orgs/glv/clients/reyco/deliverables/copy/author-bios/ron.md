## Bio

Ron works the parts counter at Reyco Marine & Small Engine. Walk-in, phone call, email, special order — if it's a parts inquiry, Ron is one of the two people on the other end (Lynn manages the room; Ron handles the counter).

Day to day, the job is a moving target. Someone walks in with a worn auger belt and the model number rubbed off the tag — Ron figures out the model from the photo and the symptom and pulls the right part. Someone calls about a Mercury water pump impeller and needs to know whether they should also replace the housing — Ron checks the parts diagram, looks at the unit's age, and gives a real recommendation instead of just shipping what was asked for. Someone emails with a snowblower part number that's been superseded twice — Ron tracks down the current OEM equivalent and confirms it fits.

The shop's "live inventory" advantage shows up most at this counter. When you call to ask if we have something, Ron checks stock in real time — not "let me see and call you back tomorrow." If we have it, you can pick it up the same day. If we don't, Ron quotes a real lead time before you commit.

Ron works alongside Lynn on parts and stays in close contact with Lee and Damian on the service bench so that the parts being pulled match what the techs actually need.

For parts: call **705-253-7828**, email **parts@reycomarine.com**, or stop by the [parts counter](/service/order-parts/) on White Oak Drive.
