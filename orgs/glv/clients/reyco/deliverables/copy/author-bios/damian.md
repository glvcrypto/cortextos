## Bio

Damian runs the small engine bench at Reyco Marine & Small Engine. Lawn mowers, snowblowers, chainsaws, trimmers, blowers — anything with a small engine that needs a tune-up or a repair lands on Damian's table.

The bench runs on a two-peak schedule. Spring brings the [lawn equipment](/service/lawn-equipment/) wave — mowers that didn't start after sitting all winter, riding mowers that need a deck and blade service, trimmers and blowers coming out of garage storage. Fall brings the [snowblower](/service/snow-equipment/) wave — annual service before the first dump, fuel system checks, belt and friction wheel inspections, the routine that keeps a unit from quitting on a 6 AM January morning. Year-round, the [small engine](/service/small-engine/) bench handles chainsaws, string trimmers and blowers — the handheld gear that contractors and homeowners both rely on.

Reyco is an authorised dealer for Cub Cadet and Toro on the lawn and snow side, and Echo on handheld — which means [warranty work](/service/warranty/) on those brands is handled here directly. (Skipping annual service or going to a non-authorised shop is what causes warranty trouble; Damian's bench is the warranty-compliant version.)

Damian works closely with Lynn and Ron on parts — the OEM items the small engine bench moves through most often (oil filters, plugs, belts, blades, shear pins, scraper bars, drive belts) are stocked because of how often they get pulled. He also coordinates with Lee on the marine bench when a customer brings in mixed equipment for service together.

To book small engine, lawn or snow equipment service, call **705-253-7828** or stop by **11 White Oak Drive East**.
