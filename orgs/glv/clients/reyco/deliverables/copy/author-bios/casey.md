## Bio

Casey leads Reyco Marine & Small Engine as owner and the face of the sales floor. If you've walked through the door looking for a new boat, mower or snowblower, chances are Casey is the first person you talked to.

His role runs the full length of a customer's journey with the shop. The first conversation about what you actually need. The package — boat, motor, trailer, electronics, accessories — built around that need rather than what's easiest to push. The handoff to the parts team and the service bench when the unit is ready. And the call back the following season to make sure everything's still running the way it should.

Casey's approach is the one most regulars know us for: ask what the gear is going to be used for, listen to the answer, then size the package to fit. The boat that fits your lake. The mower that fits your lot. The snowblower that fits your driveway. None of the rest matters if the unit doesn't match the job.

He works alongside Aaron (co-owner) and Kory on the sales floor, with Lynn and Ron handling parts and Lee and Damian on the service bench — the same small crew, season after season, all under one roof on White Oak Drive.

When you call **705-253-7828** about anything bigger than a parts order, Casey is the person you're most likely to end up talking to.
