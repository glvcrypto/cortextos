## Bio

Kory works the sales floor at Reyco Marine & Small Engine alongside Casey and Aaron. The three of them split coverage across the floor depending on what's walking through the door — boats, mowers, snowblowers, ATVs, side-by-sides, accessories, parts conversations that turn into bigger purchases.

Kory's role on the floor is the same conversation, repeated across product lines. What's the gear going to be used for? What lake, what lot, what trail, what driveway? What did the last unit do well and what did it do poorly? Once those answers are on the table, the package usually picks itself — and the conversation moves to the things that actually matter (the right motor for the boat, the right deck size for the lot, the right tire setup for the trail).

The thing customers tend to mention is the no-upsell tone. If a smaller boat fits the way the customer actually uses the lake, that's the boat Kory will recommend. If a push mower handles the lot fine, no one's getting talked into a riding mower. The shop is small enough that a customer who walks out with the wrong package is a customer who calls back unhappy in spring — which is its own kind of upsell-disincentive.

Kory works closely with the parts team (Lynn, Ron) on package add-ons and with Lee and Damian on delivery prep when a sale ties into a service item.
