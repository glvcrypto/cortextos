# Reyco Marine, Sault Ste. Marie's Marine and Small Engine Dealer, Launches New Website

*For immediate release, Sault Ste. Marie, ON*

Reyco Marine, a full-service marine and small engine dealership on White Oak Drive in Sault Ste. Marie, has launched a new website at reycomarine.com. Owner Casey Davieaux built the business to serve local customers across marine repair, small engine service, lawn, snow, and ATV/UTV equipment, with authorised brands and in-house technicians under one roof in the Sault.

Reyco Marine opened on White Oak Drive in 2022. In just over three years, the shop grew from a small engine repair and parts operation into a multi-brand authorised dealership covering marine, lawn, snow, ATV, and UTV equipment. By 2025, Reyco had secured dealer agreements with 11 brands across marine, power equipment, electronics, and utility vehicles. That pace of growth reflects both the demand for a full-service outdoor power dealer in Sault Ste. Marie and Casey Davieaux's approach to building the business: add the brands and services that Sault customers actually need, one authorised relationship at a time.

## A Full Range of Services for Every Season

Reyco Marine handles service and repair across marine engines, small engines, lawn care equipment, snow blowers, and ATVs and UTVs. The shop's workload shifts with the seasons in a way that keeps the bays running year-round. April and May bring the first push of marine repair as Sault Ste. Summer keeps the shop busy with in-season repairs, parts orders, and warranty work for customers who want to stay on the water rather than wait in a queue. Fall brings winterization for marine equipment, storage preparation, and the first wave of snow blower tune-ups as temperatures drop. From November through March, the service calendar fills with snow blower repairs, engine overhauls, and small engine work that sat all summer and needs attention before the first snowfall arrives.
