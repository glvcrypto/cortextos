## 3. Long description (250–400 words)

If you've been looking for a clean aluminum bowrider that handles lake days, fishing runs, and the odd weekend at the cottage, this 2021 Princecraft Sport 172 Max is worth a look.

It's a 17.5 foot Princecraft, all-welded aluminum hull, built in Princeville Quebec by one of Canada's longest-running aluminum boat builders. The Sport 172 Max sits in their performance line, so you're getting a hull set up to plane quick and run cleaner across chop than the entry-level lineup. The black and red colour package on this one looks sharp on the water and on the trailer.

Inside, classic bowrider seating opens up the bow for two adults up front, helm and passenger console amidships, and an aft bench that lifts for stowage. Plenty of room for a Saturday on Lake Superior or a tubing run on Lake George. Three or four anglers can fish comfortably out of this hull with rods up in the bow and off the stern.

Mercury outboard mounted, horsepower to be confirmed on Jak's walkthrough. Low hours, exact count available on request. Recently serviced and winterized in our shop at Reyco Marine in Sault Ste. Marie, so the maintenance side is current.

A few things to call Reyco about directly: current asking price, trailer availability, the full options package on this specific unit, and the service history beyond our most recent work. Jak is the contact for those.

Why buy used through Reyco: every boat that comes through our shop gets checked, serviced, and winterized by our marine techs before it goes back on the water. We're an authorized Mercury dealer, so warranty and parts on the outboard route through us cleanly. And if you're local to Sault Ste. Marie or anywhere across Algoma, you've got a real shop a short drive away if anything comes up next season.

Drop by the dealership at Reyco Marine on the waterfront or call us to set up a viewing.
