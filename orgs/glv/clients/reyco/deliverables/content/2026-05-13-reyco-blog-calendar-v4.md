## Strategy Framework

**Structure:** Category authority depth, not calendar months. Teach least-experienced reader first. Advance gradually to expert-level content.

**Frequency:** 12 articles/month minimum (3/week). Target: 5-7/week.

**Skyscraper/City Skyline sequence (mandatory):**
1. Small (1,000 words): specific subtopic article
2. Medium (3,000 words): subcategory guide (aggregates multiple small articles as sections)
3. Large (10,000 words): comprehensive category compilation (aggregates medium articles as H2 sections)
Publish SMALL first, then MEDIUM once small articles are live, then LARGE once medium articles are live. Large articles are compilations, not original research.

**Category ratio:**
- Marine: 50%
- Lawn and Garden: ~17%
- Snow Equipment: ~17%
- Power Equipment and ATV/UTV: ~17%

**Cannibalization prevention:** TOFU/MOFU/BOFU keyword differentiation inherently prevents cannibalization. No additional rules needed.

**Internal linking rule:** One rule only. Links must help the reader navigate to more valuable information. Drop arbitrary quantity limits. Drop strict orphan-page avoidance.

## Funnel Structure (per avatar)

- **TOFU (Top of Funnel):** Beginner-level awareness topics. Broadest audience.
- **MOFU (Middle of Funnel):** Comparison, extra information, deeper research topics.
- **BOFU (Bottom of Funnel):** Purchase decision topics. Closest to sale.

Each avatar gets TOFU, MOFU, and BOFU articles. Not weighted equally: Buyer avatar is BOFU-heavy; Browser avatar is TOFU-heavy.
