## 6. Priority Ranking: Red First, Then Yellow

### RED (Fix immediately)

**FIX-0009 + FIX-0010: Homepage title tag + missing meta description**

This is the highest-priority fix in this audit. The homepage has 830+ impressions in 7 days at position 6.2, the most-served page on the site. A weak title ("Home - Reyco Marine & Small Engine Ltd.") and missing meta description are suppressing clicks from every impression served.

**Proposed homepage title (155 chars):**
`Boat Dealer Sault Ste. Marie | Mercury, Princecraft, Hisun | Reyco Marine`

**Proposed homepage meta description (155 chars):**
`Northern Ontario's marine-first dealer. Mercury outboards, Princecraft boats, Hisun ATVs, small engine service. Visit us at 11 White Oak Dr. E., SSM. Call (705) 253-7828.`

**FIX-0007 / FIX-0011: /about/ meta description, brand-confusion disambiguation**

285 impressions at pos 4.8 with 0.7% CTR. This page is ranking for "superior marine sault" (competitor query) and "reyco automotive" (unrelated business). The auto-generated snippet does not disambiguate. Needs a meta description that tells users immediately: marine dealer, not automotive, not Superior Marine.

**Proposed /about/ meta description:**
`Reyco Marine & Small Engine is Sault Ste. Marie's authorised boat and motor dealer, not Reyco Automotive. 28,000 sq ft facility, year-round service, authorised Mercury dealer.`

### YELLOW (Fix this week)

**FIX-0012: /service/small-engine/ title tag**

Current: `Small Engine Repair - Reyco Marine & Small Engine Ltd.`
Issue: H1 includes "Sault Ste. Marie" but title does not. Title = stronger ranking signal than H1.

Proposed: `Small Engine Repair in Sault Ste. Marie | Reyco Marine`

**FIX-0013: Princecraft inventory page returning empty**

This is a product/dealer page for SSM's only Princecraft dealer. Ranking at position 22.8 with 125 impressions, the brand search intent is real. If the page returns empty results, users bounce immediately. Check if /search/inventory/Brand/Princecraft (capital B) serves correctly vs /brand/Princecraft (lowercase). If URL case is the issue, add a redirect. If it's a WooCommerce filter config, dev fix needed.

**FIX-0003, FIX-0005, FIX-0006, FIX-0008: Dev batch**

All four confirmed for dev as of May 11. Target: single PR covering www canonical redirect, UTM canonicalization, faceted nav noindex, EZGO generator filter noindex. No new urgency, carry-over.
