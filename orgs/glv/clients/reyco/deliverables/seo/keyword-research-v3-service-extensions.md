## Intent Flags (Pre-Work — Read Before Building Pages)

These four clusters have split or ambiguous intent that will affect page structure and targeting strategy:

| Page | Intent Risk | Recommendation |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Engine Repair | "engine repair near me" is dominated by automotive (cars/trucks). Reyco does NOT repair car engines. | Target equipment-specific modifiers throughout: "outboard engine repair", "small engine repair", "power equipment engine repair". Avoid ranking for bare "engine repair" — wrong audience. |
| Tune Ups | Three distinct searcher sub-intents: (1) lawn/OPE tune-up, (2) snowblower tune-up, (3) outboard tune-up. A single page may appear thin to Google across all three. | Options: hub page with H2 sections per equipment type (thin but fast) OR three separate seasonal pages (best long-term, more build time). Keyword research below supports hub structure. |
| Order Parts | Transactional primary ("buy parts now", Lightspeed live inventory) vs informational secondary ("can I order parts for X here?"). | Build transactional-first: clear "order now" CTA, part number search, brand filters. Informational intent handled with FAQ block below the fold. |
| Warranty Claims | Manufacturer-specific queries dominate ("Mercury warranty claim", "Toro warranty Canada"). Reyco's value is being the authorized LOCAL contact — you don't file warranty claims yourself, you bring the equipment to Reyco. | Page framing: "Reyco handles your warranty repair — bring it in, we do the rest." Per-brand FAQ blocks capture brand-specific query traffic. |
