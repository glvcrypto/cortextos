## 2. Meta Title

**Recommendation (60 chars):**
```
Reyco Marine & Small Engine | Sault Ste. Marie
```

**Rationale:**
- Brand name first — branded searches are the highest-conversion query type
- Location in title — captures city + brand co-searches, local pack signal
- No service category stuffing — homepage isn't the conversion page for service terms; service pages handle that

**Alt option if dev needs to test a richer variant (65 chars):**
```
Reyco Marine | Boat, Mower & Engine Dealer — Sault Ste. Marie
```
*Use if CTR testing shows the category signal improves click-through. Primary recommendation stands as the cleaner brand-first option.*

## 5. Page Section Brief

### Section 1 — Hero
**Goal:** Immediately communicate what Reyco is + where they are + how to reach them.  
**Elements:**
- H1 (above)
- Subhead (above)
- 2 primary CTAs: "Browse Inventory" + "Book Service"
- Phone number visible (705-253-7828)

**No long body copy in hero** — homepage visitors are navigating, not reading. Get them to the right section or service page fast.

---

### Section 2 — What We Do (4-panel)
**Goal:** Surface the four revenue categories with keyword signal and internal links.

| Panel | Heading | Link target | 1-line copy angle |
|-------|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Sales | Boats, Mowers & More | /products/ or /inventory/ | New and in-stock — packages built around how you'll actually use the gear |
| Service | Service & Repair | /service/ | All bench work done in-house by the same techs every season |
| Parts | Parts Counter | /parts/ | OEM parts for every brand we carry, stocked on-site |
| Authorized Dealer | Mercury · Cub Cadet · Toro · Echo | /service/marine/ | Factory-authorized warranty service — no shipping back to head office |

**SEO note:** Each panel heading is an H2. "Service & Repair" in the heading + a short sentence mentioning the top service terms (boats, mowers, snowblowers, small engines) puts those terms on the homepage without targeting them directly.

---

### Section 3 — Service Categories (Cards)
**Goal:** Direct service-intent visitors to the right service page. Homepage isn't the conversion page — service pages are.

**Recommended cards (in order of national search volume from v4):**
1. Small Engine Repair → `/service/small-engine/` (3,600/mo national intent pool)
2. Lawn Equipment Service → `/service/lawn-equipment/` (1,300/mo)
3. Snow Equipment Service → `/service/snow-equipment/` (1,600/mo)
4. Marine Service → `/service/marine/` (590/mo boat repair)
5. ATV/UTV Repair → `/service/atv-utv-repair/` (590/mo ATV repair)
6. Tune-Ups → `/service/tune-ups/`
7. Order Parts → `/parts/`

**Copy for each card:** 1 sentence only — what the service is + who it's for. No need for keyword-dense copy at the card level; the link equity flows to the service page which has the full content.

---

### Section 4 — About / Social Proof Teaser
**Goal:** E-E-A-T signal — establish that Reyco is local, established, and staffed by real people.

**Copy direction:**
- "More than 60 years serving Sault Ste. Marie" — business longevity signal
- Small crew photo or team teaser CTA → `/about/` (Meet the Team page)
- Authorized dealer badge strip (Mercury, Princecraft, Cub Cadet, Toro, Echo logos)

**SEO note:** The authorized dealer badge strip is the homepage's primary brand-signal section. Google's entity recognition for "authorized Mercury dealer" connects the brand associations. Alt text on each logo = "[Brand] authorized dealer Sault Ste. Marie".

## 7. Schema Note

LocalBusiness schema (already built — see `schema/schema-markup-templates.md`) should be placed on the homepage, not as a sitewide injection. The homepage is the canonical `@id` reference for the business entity. All other pages' schemas that include `"worksFor"` or `"@id": "https://reycomarine.com/#business"` point back here.

**No additional schema required on the homepage.** LocalBusiness covers it.

## 8. What's NOT Needed

- No long-form body copy on the homepage. It's a hub, not a landing page.
- No blog feed or news section — Reyco has no blog and this doesn't need to be built at launch.
- No product carousels unless the WC inventory is fully seeded — a sparse carousel hurts more than helps.
- No schema for "WebSite" or "Organization" — LocalBusiness supersedes both in this use case.
