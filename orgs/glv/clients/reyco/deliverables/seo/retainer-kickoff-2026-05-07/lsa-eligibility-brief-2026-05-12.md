## What Are Local Services Ads?

Local Services Ads (LSA) are Google-managed pay-per-lead ad units that appear **above** the organic local 3-pack on local service searches. They carry a "Google Guaranteed" badge, include click-to-call, and charge per verified lead (not per click).

**Why this matters now:** Sterling Sky's State of Local SEO 2026 reports LSA units now appear on **31% of tracked local queries**, up from 11% in early 2025. For trade and service businesses, this is the single biggest shift in local SERP real estate in 2–3 years.

## What LSA Gets Reyco

- **Position:** Above the organic 3-pack — first result on ~1 in 3 local service queries
- **Badge:** "Google Guaranteed" — Google backs a lifetime cap ($2,000 CAD) on verified complaints; strong trust signal for first-time customers
- **Billing:** Pay-per-lead (not per click) — typically $15–$45 CAD per verified lead for marine/small engine service in ON mid-markets
- **No long-term commitment:** Budget is fully flexible; can pause/adjust anytime
