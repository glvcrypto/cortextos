1. Fix existing on-page gaps (meta descriptions, schema, internal links)
2. Publish 4 high-priority pages that own uncontested KD=0 local terms
3. Establish blog with 3–4 authoritative posts before expanding volume

Every piece of content targets a specific keyword gap at KD=0 locally. No generic brand content. No product descriptions. Every URL should answer a question someone in SSM is actually searching.

## Content Quality Bar

Every piece published must pass the "Next 3 Questions" test (March 2026 Core Update framework):
1. What's the primary question this content answers?
2. What are the 3 questions a user would ask next after reading this?
3. Does this content link to pages that answer those next questions, OR are those answers included?

If the answer to #3 is no on both counts, the content is not ready to publish.
