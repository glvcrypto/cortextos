# Mercury Outboard Service in Sault Ste. Marie

Lake Superior covers 82,000 square kilometres — more surface area than any freshwater lake on earth. For boaters running Mercury outboards on Superior, the St. Marys River, or the inland lakes of the Algoma district, the question of who services your motor is not a preference. It is a warranty decision.

Reyco Marine is an authorised Mercury dealer in Sault Ste. Marie. That authorisation determines what your warranty covers and who is qualified to work on your motor under it.

---

## What Mercury Authorisation Means for You

Mercury's factory warranty requires that warranty service be performed by an authorised dealer with factory-trained technicians. Service done outside the authorised network — even by a competent independent shop — voids coverage for the work performed and can affect related systems. If your motor is under warranty and something goes wrong after an unauthorised service, Mercury's obligation ends at the dealer's door.

Reyco's authorised status covers the full Mercury line: portable outboards, mid-range four-strokes, and high-output engines used in tournament and offshore applications. Our service scope includes factory warranty repairs, post-warranty service, and Mercury-certified maintenance intervals.

## The Service Team

Lee, Damian, and Cody perform all Mercury outboard work at our Sault Ste. Marie facility. All three are factory-trained on current Mercury product lines. Lynn manages our Mercury OEM parts inventory — we stock common service items (impellers, filters, spark plugs, anodes, propellers) and can order less common parts from Mercury directly, with most ordered parts arriving within a few business days.

Aaron manages our service scheduling. If your motor needs a written estimate before work begins — for warranty submissions or your own records — Aaron will provide one before we proceed.

---

## If You Didn't Buy Your Motor Here

Authorised service is not limited to motors sold by Reyco. If you purchased a Mercury through another dealer, a private sale, or an online platform, we can service it under warranty provided the warranty is still valid and the work falls within our authorised scope. Call the service desk with your motor's serial number and model year and we will confirm eligibility before you make the drive.

For motors outside warranty, we handle all Mercury non-warranty service and repairs at the same facility, with the same team.

---

## Book Service

Call our service desk to book. Have your motor's serial number, model, and year ready — it allows us to confirm parts availability and provide an accurate timeline before your appointment. For parts inquiries, contact Lynn or Ron at the parts counter directly.

Reyco Marine has been the Algoma district's authorised Mercury service point for more than 40 years.
