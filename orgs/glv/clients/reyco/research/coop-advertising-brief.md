## Brand-by-Brand Detail

### Mercury Marine ⭐ (Reyco's primary engine brand)

**Rate:** 50% reimbursement for Canadian dealers (non-committed tier)
**Accrual:** Based on qualifying product purchases; tracked in MercNET
**Eligible media:** Digital, print, radio, TV, cable, outdoor. Products must be marked "US & CAN" co-op eligible.
**Claim process:**
1. Purchase co-op eligible products at mercurymarketingondemand.com
2. Download invoice
3. Submit via MercNET → Sales → Co-op Advertising → Co-op Advertising Entry
**Prior approval:** Required for all ads
**Action for Reyco:** Confirm Casey/Tyler have MercNET access and know how to access the co-op section. Check current accrual balance. Start with digital ads (website, social) which are clearly eligible.

### Cub Cadet

**Rate:** ~50% (confirmed program, exact rate varies by tier)
**Platform:** cubcadetus.com, administered by Mediagistic
**Requirements:** Cub Cadet products must be featured in ≥50% of ad content. Prior approval required.
**Eligible media:** Local newspaper, magazine, digital
**Action for Reyco:** Register/verify dealer account at cubcadetus.com to check accrual balance and request pre-approval before next spring lawn care campaign.

---

### Toro

**Rate:** 50–70% reimbursement
**Platform:** Co-op Connect (coopconnect.com)
**Requirements:** Prior approval required for all ads. Toro logo/branding must be prominent.
**Eligible media:** Lawn & Garden category programs — print, digital, outdoor
**Action for Reyco:** Access Toro dealer portal to verify eligibility and check Co-op Connect enrollment.
