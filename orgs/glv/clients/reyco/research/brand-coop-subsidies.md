## What Is Co-Op Advertising?

Co-op (cooperative advertising) programs allow manufacturers to reimburse a percentage of a dealer's qualified advertising spend. The dealer pays upfront, submits a claim with proof of performance, and receives a rebate. Funds typically accrue as a percentage of product purchases (1–3% is industry standard) and have a "use it or lose it" expiration (usually 6–12 months).

**The "80% rebate" figure in the brief** most likely refers to co-op programs where the brand covers 50–100% of eligible advertising spend — not a discount off product prices. This is standard for committed dealers at premium program tiers.
