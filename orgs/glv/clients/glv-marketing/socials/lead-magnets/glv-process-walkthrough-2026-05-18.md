# How GLV Works: The Five-Step Process

**glvmarketing.ca**

---

Every GLV client goes through the same structured process. Not because it's easier for us. Because it's what actually produces results that compound month over month.

Here's exactly what each step looks like.

---

## Step 1: We Research Your Market First

Before we touch your website or write a word of content, we learn your market.

That means your competitive landscape, who's ranking for the terms your customers search and what they're doing well. Your target search terms at the intent level, not just the volume level. What your customers need to see before they'll pick up the phone.

That research drives every decision downstream. Get it right and everything that follows is better calibrated.

**Timeline:** Week 1. Nothing ships before this is done.

---

## Step 2: We Build the Foundation

Once we know the landscape, we build.

Website structure, content plan, SEO architecture, local presence setup. In the right order. Foundation before content. Structure before campaigns.

The order matters more than most people realise. A well-structured site makes content work harder. A strong local presence makes paid ads more efficient. You can't stack things that don't have a base under them.

**Timeline:** Weeks 2-4. Varies by scope of existing assets.

---

## Step 3: We Execute Consistently

The foundation is only worth what you put on top of it. And that work has to be consistent.

Content goes out on schedule. SEO work gets done weekly, not in bursts before a quarterly report. Ads get monitored and adjusted on a regular cadence. No off weeks. No stretches where nothing moves forward.

Consistency is one of the most underrated factors in marketing results. Google rewards sites that publish on a cadence. Email lists decay if they go quiet. The businesses that compound are the ones where the work doesn't stop.

**Timeline:** Ongoing from month 1. This is the core of the retainer.

---

## Step 4: We Review Before It Ships

Nothing goes out without a review step.

Strategy calls before major decisions. Content sign-off before it's published. Campaign reports before the next period is planned.

That review layer is what keeps the strategy aligned with what's actually happening in your business. You might have a product launch coming, a seasonal push, something that shifts the priority. The review step is where that information gets in.

**Timeline:** Regular cadence throughout. You're never out of the loop.

---

## Step 5: Watch It Compound

It isn't dramatic in any single month.

Month one, you've got a foundation. Month three, a few pages are ranking. Month six, organic traffic is up and you're getting leads you didn't have before.

By month 12 the marketing is working in a fundamentally different way than it was at the start. The content is indexed and earning. The local presence is established. The campaigns are optimised on real data.

This is what it looks like when the work stacks instead of resets.

**Timeline:** Compounding becomes visible around month 3-4. Significant by month 6.

---

## What Makes This Different

Most agencies figure it out as they go. Each month feels like starting over because there's no underlying structure making the work stack.

The reason GLV's process works is that every step is designed to make the next one more effective. The research makes the build better. The build makes the execution more efficient. The consistency makes the results compound. The review keeps it all calibrated.

One team, one strategy, every step connected.
