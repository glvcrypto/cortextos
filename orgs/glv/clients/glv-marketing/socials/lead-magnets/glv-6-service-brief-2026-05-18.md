## 1. Local SEO and Google Business Profile

Most local businesses are invisible on Google Maps. Wrong categories, no review strategy, missing service areas. That's costing you calls you don't know you're missing.

We audit your GBP, fix what's broken, build out your local content, and optimise your profile on a regular cadence. The goal is showing up when someone in your city searches what you do.

**What changes:** More calls from Google Maps. Organic traffic from people who are already looking for you.

---

## 2. Website Design and Development

Your website is either earning leads or losing them. Most local business sites are slow, hard to navigate on mobile, and not built for search.

We build on WordPress and Vite. Clean structure, fast load times, built around the search terms your customers actually use. No page-builder bloat.

**What changes:** A site that works as a sales tool, not just a brochure.

---

## 3. Email Marketing

Email is the only channel you actually own. No algorithm can take it away.

We set up your list, build out your welcome sequence, and run ongoing campaigns that keep your audience warm between purchases or visits. Not a MailChimp template. Written copy that sounds like you.

**What changes:** A list that earns on its own. Customers who come back without being chased.

---

## 4. Paid Ads (Google and Meta)

Ads are the accelerant. They work best when the foundation is already right.

We run search and display campaigns on Google and paid social on Meta. Targeted by location, intent, and audience. We monitor weekly, adjust based on data, and don't set campaigns to auto-run and forget.

**What changes:** Leads that come in on a schedule. Ad spend that's accountable.

---

## 5. AI Integration

Most small businesses aren't using AI in their operations yet. The ones that are are moving faster and spending less on repetitive tasks.

We identify where AI tools fit in your workflow, set them up, and train your team on using them. Not theoretical. Specific tools for specific jobs.

**What changes:** Time back in your week. Less manual work on things a system can handle.

---

## 6. Local AI for Regulated Industries

For businesses in law, medicine, finance, and other regulated sectors, cloud-based AI tools create compliance and privacy risks. Data leaves your building. That's not acceptable in every industry.

We set up and run AI systems that stay on your hardware. No data sent to third-party servers. Private by design.

**What changes:** The productivity benefits of AI without the compliance exposure.
