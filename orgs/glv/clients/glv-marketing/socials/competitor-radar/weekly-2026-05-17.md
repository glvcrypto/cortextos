## Executive Summary

1. **GLV's most direct local competitor (DIG/digdeep.ca) has zero active social presence.** Their website copyright is 2022, no social links exist anywhere on the site, and all platform searches returned dormant or inaccessible profiles. The SSM digital marketing social space is wide open for GLV to claim and own without competition.

2. **Neil Patel posts 5-7x/week across 5+ platforms** — the national thought-leader benchmark operates at a volume GLV cannot match. GLV's counter-strategy: hyper-local specificity (SSM/Northern Ontario framing) on topics Patel covers nationally. Own the local version of what he owns nationally.

3. **Search Engine Land has shifted from real-time X/Twitter to newsletter-first.** Their 454K X following is legacy — posting cadence has slowed significantly. Industry signal for GLV now lives in SEL's weekly newsletter, not their social feed.

4. **Village Media / SooToday is a PR channel, not just a benchmark.** Their Spotlight and Community Leaders Program advertiser packages make SooToday a potential distribution channel for GLV clients — especially trades, retail, and professional services looking for local brand exposure.

5. **Local SMB digital marketing education is completely unclaimed in SSM.** DIG doesn't produce educational content. Village Media doesn't produce marketing how-to content. Neil Patel is national-scale. GLV has an uncontested lane to be THE local marketing authority in SSM.
