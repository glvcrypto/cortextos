## Season Summary (Feb–April Campaign Arc)

This campaign launched in February 2026 targeting Sault Ste. Marie's tax season window.

**Key events:**
- **Feb 20:** Campaign launched with 11 ads at ~$17.56/day. Algorithm selected SSM Day and Envelope as winners.
- **Feb 23:** Campaign went dark — 5 consecutive days with zero paid social sessions.
- **March rebuild:** Restructured to 2 active ads at $15/day through April 30.
- **April:** Final tax season push through filing deadline.

**Feb campaign learnings carried into April:**
- SSM Day (local angle) and Envelope (procrastination/relief) were the proven creative hooks
- Broad targeting outperformed interest-based in the SSM market size
- Lead form simplicity is critical — mobile load time and field count affect conversion rate
