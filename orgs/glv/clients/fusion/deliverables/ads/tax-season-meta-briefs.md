## Brief 1: SSM Day (Refresh)

**Ad Set:** A - Tax Season (Higher Intent)
**Budget allocation:** $17.50/day
**Why this ad:** Generated the only lead in the Feb campaign. Highest spend ($44.43). Has a Quality Ranking from Meta. This is the proven winner.

**Headline:** Get Your Taxes Done Right in Sault Ste. Marie
**Primary Text:**
Tax season is here. Whether you are filing personal taxes or need your business books sorted before April 30, Fusion Financial handles it all.

We are a local Sault Ste. Marie firm that knows CRA inside and out. No guessing, no stress, no surprises.

Book a free consultation today.

**CTA Button:** Book Now
**Landing:** fusionfinancialssm.com (lead form)
**Creative direction:** Photo of downtown SSM or local landmark. Warm, professional tones. Fusion Financial branding (navy #1A3A5C). Avoid stock photos that feel corporate or American.
**Target audience:** Sault Ste. Marie area, ages 28-55, broad targeting (let Meta's algorithm find the audience)

**Refresh notes vs. original:**
- Tighten the copy (original was longer)
- Add the April 30 deadline urgency
- Keep the local SSM angle that made it work the first time

## Brief 3: Checklist (New Angle)

**Ad Set:** B - Ongoing Services (Awareness)
**Budget allocation:** $15/day
**Why this ad:** Lead-magnet angle (checklist resource) is a different hook. Lower intent but builds the retargeting audience. Worth testing at scale.

**Headline:** Free Tax Prep Checklist for Sault Ste. Marie Residents
**Primary Text:**
Not sure if you have everything you need for tax season? We put together a quick checklist of the documents and deadlines every Sault Ste. Marie resident should know about before filing.

Download it free. No obligation.

If you need help after that, we are right here in the Soo.

**CTA Button:** Download
**Landing:** fusionfinancialssm.com (lead form with checklist offer)
**Creative direction:** Clean, simple design. Checklist visual (checkmarks, organized list). Green accent colour for "completed" feel. Fusion Financial branding.
**Target audience:** Slightly broader than Ad Set A. Sault Ste. Marie + surrounding area, ages 25-60. Include interests: small business owners, self-employed, freelancers.

**Performance trigger:** If Checklist generates fewer than 1 lead by March 15 (10 days), pause it and move the $15/day to Ad Set A.
