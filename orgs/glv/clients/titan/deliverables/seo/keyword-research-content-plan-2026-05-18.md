## Strategic Context

Titan Tiny Homes operates in a high-growth category (tiny homes / ADUs / alternative housing) with a national + international audience but local-first operational roots (SSM / Northern Ontario delivery zone). The SEO strategy must serve two distinct buyer journeys simultaneously:

1. **Local delivery zone buyers** (SSM, Northern ON) — high purchase intent, short funnel, price-sensitive
2. **National + international dreamers and researchers** (Canada-wide, US, EU) — long funnel, high content engagement, lower near-term conversion but strong brand building and referral traffic

The 3-axis matrix below structures keywords across these journeys.
