### What the GSC data reveals

1. **Brand traffic dominates.** "titan tiny homes" alone = 68 clicks at 40.7% CTR. The brand is searchable and converts when found.
2. **Brand-name collision with Titan Factory Direct (Texas).** "titan factory direct canada" pulled 213 impressions but only 2.3% CTR. Searchers want the Texas brand and bounce when they realize this is a different company. This is non-brand impression bloat; it inflates average position numbers without converting.
3. **Local owns a tiny but high-CTR niche.** "tiny homes sault ste marie" at position 2.3 with 29.4% CTR is the strongest non-brand performance. SSM-local SEO is a defensible moat.
4. **Page 2 ceiling.** Average position 18.3 across the site means most non-brand queries surface but don't click. Every position improvement from 11-20 to 5-10 typically triples CTR.
5. **Educational content is absent.** Queries like "buy a tiny home canada" (pos 67), "buy tiny house" (pos 72), "cabin tiny homes for sale" (pos 64) show informational searches where Titan ranks past page 5 because no content targets those searchers.

## Strategic Recommendations Grounded in the Data

### 1. Lock the brand SERP first

Two-hour fix. The query "titan tiny homes" sits at position 8.9 with 40.7% CTR. A homepage title tag pass and a Brand schema review should pull this to position 1-3.

- Confirm title tag is "Titan Tiny Homes | [tagline]" not generic
- Add Organization + LocalBusiness schema with logo, sameAs (social), contactPoint
- Build out /about page (currently 2 impressions, 1 click) into a proper brand hub

### 2. Disambiguate from Titan Factory Direct

That 213-impression bleed on "titan factory direct canada" pulls average position down and adds zero conversion. Two options:

- **Mention the difference once on the homepage** in a small disclaimer or FAQ ("Not affiliated with Titan Factory Direct of Texas")
- **Or accept and ignore.** The traffic is non-converting and ignoring it does not hurt the site, but it does muddy GSC dashboards. Recommendation: short FAQ entry, no schema work.

### 3. Own "Ontario tiny homes" as the long-term moat

The data says Ontario is fully open. SEMrush shows Mint, Rewild, Sunshine, Summit all ranking for BC terms with thousands of monthly searches. No competitor ranks well for "ontario tiny home" (210/mo), "tiny homes for sale ontario" (1,600/mo), or "tiny home builders ontario" (320/mo). Titan currently sits at positions 57, 43, 72 respectively.

**Quick math:** Moving "tiny homes for sale ontario" from position 43 to position 5 on a 1,600/mo query at ~6-8% CTR = ~100 clicks/mo from a single keyword.

The Ontario regional play is the highest-ROI 12-month direction.

### 4. Build the local SSM moat

"tiny homes sault ste marie" already ranks position 2.3 with 29.4% CTR. The volume is small (34 impressions/90d) but the conversion intent is high and competitors are not contesting it. Reinforce with:

- /sault-ste-marie landing page (NAP, delivery zone map, service radius)
- LocalBusiness schema with Sault Ste Marie address
- Northern Ontario service-area expansion pages (Sudbury, Timmins, North Bay, Thunder Bay)

This is small absolute volume, but every click here is a buyer.

### 5. Content gaps from GSC

GSC shows Titan ranking pos 60-72 for queries that should pull good traffic:

| Query | Impr | Current pos | Volume tier | Opportunity |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| buy a tiny home canada | 2 | 67.5 | 90/mo | Buying guide for Canadian regs |
| buy tiny home ontario | 1 | 48 | 30/mo | Ontario-specific buying guide |
| buy tiny house | 1 | 72 | 720/mo | National buying guide |
| cabin tiny homes for sale | 1 | 64 | small | Cabin variant content |
| turn key tiny homes for sale | 1 | 1.0 | tiny | Already winning, expand cluster |

Titan ranks #1 for "turn key tiny homes for sale" already (1 click on 1 impression) but the query has near-zero volume. The buying-guide cluster is where the volume sits and where the rankings are weakest.
