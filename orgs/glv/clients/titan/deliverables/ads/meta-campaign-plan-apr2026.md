## Campaign Structure

Meta's Andromeda update means **creative is now the primary targeting signal** — not audience settings. The algorithm reads your creative and finds the right people. Implication: we need volume of creative, not precision targeting.

### Campaign 1: Creative Testing ($6/day — ~$180/mo)
**Purpose:** Feed new creative variants to the algorithm. Discover what hooks drive leads at lowest CPL.

- Ad set: Broad — Canada, 25–55, no interest stacking
- 3–5 active ads at a time, rotate every 2 weeks
- Kill anything under 1% CTR after 7 days / $50 spend
- Winners graduate to Campaign 2

### Campaign 2: Lead Scale ($10.67/day — ~$320/mo)
**Purpose:** Scale proven creative. Budget concentrates on the 1–2 ads the algorithm has validated.

- Same broad targeting
- Only creatives that cleared Campaign 1 threshold
- CPL target: $40–$80 CAD (tiny home is a high-consideration purchase — CPL will be higher than commodity products)
- Optimize for lead form opens → completions

## Targeting

**The Andromeda rule:** Don't over-target. Broad is better at this market size (all of Canada). Let the creative self-select the audience.

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Geography | Canada (all provinces) |
| Age | 28–55 |
| Gender | All |
| Interest targeting | None (broad — let creative do the work) |
| Placement | Auto (Meta optimizes across FB feed, IG feed, Stories, Reels) |

**Why broad?** Tiny home buyers don't cluster neatly in Meta's interest graph. Someone interested in "sustainable living" or "real estate" is not the same as someone actively considering a tiny home purchase. Broad + strong creative finds real buyers faster and cheaper than interest-stacked narrow audiences at Canada scale.

## Creative Strategy (UGC-First)

Per Meta Andromeda research: **UGC (raw/authentic) outperforms polished production**. Joey and Kathryn filming on their phones in the build shop is the single highest-ROI creative move available.

### Creative Tier 1: Founder UGC (Highest Priority)
Joey or Kathryn on camera. No script needed — just a phone, good light, and 30–60 seconds.

**Video prompts for Joey/Kathryn:**
1. "Walk us through the build process — start to finish in 60 seconds"
2. "Show us a unit fresh off the line. Walk through it like you're selling it to a friend."
3. "What's the #1 question you get from buyers? Answer it on camera."
4. "What makes a Titan tiny home different from what you'd find online for half the price?"

### Creative Tier 2: Static Image Ads
Single image with copy overlay. Use the AI-generated property photos already on the site.

### Creative Tier 3: Carousel
3–5 images showing different unit styles, interior shots, build process.

## Lead Form Design

**Keep it short. Mobile-first. Every extra field costs leads.**

Recommended fields:
1. First name
2. Email
3. Phone (optional — mark it clearly)
4. One qualifying question: "Do you have land, or are you still exploring?" (helps Joey prioritize follow-up)

Thank-you message: "Thanks — Joey will be in touch within 1 business day."
