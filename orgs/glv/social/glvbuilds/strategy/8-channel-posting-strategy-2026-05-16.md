## Per-channel adaptation playbook

### LinkedIn (Tier 1 — heaviest investment)
- **Format:** Native PDF carousel (6–10 slides). Same content as IG slides but text density can run higher per page. Cover slide is the feed thumbnail — must read at 270×340.
- **Persona:** Aiden Glave personal profile. Reposts amplified on GLV Marketing company page. Personal:Page reach math = 62%:5%.
- **Caption:** Up to 3,000 chars, but front-load — "See more" cuts at 210–220. Lead with value, **never start with "I"** (penalized). Use the LinkedIn caption already drafted in each `2026-05-15_carousel-*.md` file.
- **Links:** Body link costs 26.5% reach. Put any link in **first comment** only.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5. Mix 1–2 broad (#LocalSEO, #DigitalMarketing) + 2–3 niche (#NorthernOntario, #SaultSteMarie, #GBP).
- **Engagement window:** Reply to comments within 30 min for +64% comments and 2.3x more views.

### Instagram (Tier 2)
- **Format:** Native IG carousel, 1080×1350px (4:5), 7 slides per existing drafts. Cover slide doubles as re-serve hook (IG re-surfaces partial swipers).
- **Caption:** 1–3 short paragraphs from the IG Caption section in each draft. First 125 chars = the preview hook.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 at end of caption. Same mix as LinkedIn (broader for IG search since hashtag-following is gone Dec 2024).
- **Posting time:** Tue/Thu 9–10 AM ET.

### Threads (Tier 3 — IG audience portability play)
- **Format:** Text-based post, 500 char max. Conversational, ends in a question or invitation to reply.
- **Adaptation:** Take the IG caption's hook + one mid-slide line + a question. Example for #1: "My first local SEO client thought his ranking problem was technical. It wasn't. The answer was a Google Business Profile that had never been claimed. What's the worst inherited GBP you've audited?"
- **Topic tag:** 1 per post (e.g., `localseo`).
- **Linked threads:** chain longer educational content with "1/" "2/" if needed.
- **Replies:** budget time to reply within 30–60 min — replies-to-replies expand the algorithm.

### TikTok (Tier 2 — needs reel content, not carousels)
- **Note:** TikTok does not natively support carousels in the same algorithm slot as Reels. The carousel image-set works but is a different ecosystem. The **5th post (5-GBP-settings)** is where TikTok becomes load-bearing — if Aiden films a 30–60s reel covering the 5 settings, that's the TikTok-native launch.
- **For posts 1–4 (carousels):** TikTok carousel image-post supported, 3–5 hashtags, opening clause keyword-first. Lower priority than LinkedIn/IG for these.
- **TikTok SEO:** Caption opening clause > on-screen text > spoken audio > hashtags. Always say + write + overlay the target keyword.
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds non-negotiable. <3s swipe = view jail.

### Facebook (Tier 3 — Reels native + Group play)
- **Format:** Album upload (1:1 crops of carousel slides). Native upload only — **never** auto-cross-post from IG (72% reach penalty per Agorapulse controlled study).
- **Caption:** Warmer/community tone vs LinkedIn. Same content idea, conversational framing.
- **Reach reality:** 1.37% organic page reach. Don't measure FB on raw numbers — measure on Reels tab discovery + Group warm leads.
- **Group play (separate, ongoing):** Create / formalize a local Group ("Northern Ontario Small Business Growth" or similar). Position as facilitator, never sell inside. Future warm lead source — not part of foundational launch but should be queued for week 4+.
- **BLOCKED:** baseline audit could not confirm page slug or current state due to FB login wall. Need Aiden to confirm page URL + status before first Blotato schedule.

### YouTube Shorts (Tier 1 — but needs video content, not carousels)
- **Launch role:** YouTube doesn't post carousels. Foundational 5 don't activate YouTube unless we film. Recommend queuing 2 YouTube Shorts for week 2–3 of launch using the existing drafts repurposed as scripts.
- **Title:** Write as search query. NOT "Growth Tip #1" — instead "How to fix a Google Business Profile (the 6-point pre-audit)".
- **Description:** Keyword-rich, link to glvmarketing.ca, 2–3 hashtags.
- **Channel setup blocker:** YouTube channel was created today (May 16). Needs description, banner, and channel art before first Short.

### X / Twitter (Tier 4 — decision needed)
- **Format:** Text thread, lead tweet 280 chars max. Sharp/contrarian framing.
- **Caveat:** Free accounts get <100 impressions median; link posts get 0% engagement. **Without X Premium ($8–22 USD/month) the channel is essentially invisible.**
- **Decision:** Aiden — (a) skip X for now (focus the time elsewhere, revisit Q3/Q4), or (b) commit to Premium + 2x/day cadence.
- **Default if no decision:** I'll post the launch threads anyway (low marginal time cost since the content is adapted from LinkedIn) but won't invest in growth here.

### GBP (Tier 4 — always-on transactional)
- **Format:** Update post, 150-char-visible hook, image (non-stock — 5.6x more clicks), CTA button (Learn more → glvmarketing.ca).
- **DO NOT:** post the carousel content directly to GBP. GBP audience is in **transactional** mode, not social-scroll. Posts that work on IG don't work on GBP.
- **Adaptation for carousel #3 (gbp-primary-category):** *"Most local businesses chose the wrong GBP primary category at signup. We'll audit yours free — 30 min, no commitment. Reply to this post or call (705) 975-0579."*
- **Cadence:** 1–3x/week. Plan for 1x Update post per week on existing carousel content (heavily adapted), 1x Offer post per month (e.g., "Free GBP visibility check — 5 spots this month").
- **GBP completeness pass (one-shot, blocking before first GBP post):** confirm address shows publicly, upload 8–12 non-stock photos, fill business description, set attributes, seed 5–8 Q&As proactively.
- **Per scout research:** do NOT connect Instagram or Facebook to GBP — the connected social feed replaces native Update posts in the profile view. Connect LinkedIn or YouTube only, or nothing.

## Hook library (per channel — pulled from research)

### LinkedIn (front-loaded value, no "I" start, 210-char hook)
- *"After managing local SEO for [N] Northern Ontario businesses, here are the 5 things that actually move the needle."*
- *"Most local business owners think their Google ranking problem is technical. It's almost never technical. Here's the real reason."*
- *"I analyzed [N] Google Business Profiles last month. 80% had the same fixable mistake. Here's the audit I run."* ← strong but starts with "I" — restructure.
- *"There's one field in Google Business Profile that carries more weight on local pack rankings than anything else. Most owners set it in 15 seconds and never look back."*

### Instagram (visual hook + first 125 chars caption)
- Lead with the cover slide claim. Caption preview line: *"My first local SEO client thought it was a website problem. It wasn't."*
- *"6 things I check before I touch a client's Google listing. 10 minutes. Every new client. Every time."*

### Threads (conversational, ends in a question)
- *"Posting 3x a week consistently beats posting daily and burning out. Change my mind."*
- *"What's the worst inherited Google Business Profile you've audited? Mine had a 2019 review and a wrong address."*
- *"Everyone tells local businesses to be on every platform. Here's why that's bad advice."*

### TikTok (first 3 seconds, pattern interrupt + curiosity)
- *"Most marketing agencies are killing their clients' Google ranking and don't even know it."*
- *"This 30-second Google Business Profile fix doubled my client's call volume."*
- *"Why do 80% of local businesses fail at Google Ads? It's not what you think."*

### YouTube Shorts (title as search query, "watch until end" premise)
- Title: *"How to set up Google Business Profile (the 6-point pre-audit)"*

- Title: *"Why your business isn't showing up on Google Maps"*

### X (sharp, contrarian, 280 chars max)
