# 2027-02-20 | LinkedIn + Instagram | Reel (~30–35s) | CTA: open comment

**Date:** February 20, 2027
**Platform:** LinkedIn + Instagram
**Content type:** Reel — ~30–35 seconds
**CTA:** Open comment (no automation)
**Topic:** GBP Products tab — writing entries that get indexed and show in local pack sidebar
**MEMORY cluster:** GBP Products tab (extends Sep 15 overview — this is the writing-entries workflow)

---

## Script

**[Screen: Google Maps — local business listing with Products section visible in the right sidebar]**

**Hook (on screen + voiceover):**
"GBP's Products tab doesn't just show what you sell. Google indexes the text in every entry. Here's how to use it as extra keyword coverage."

---

**[Screen: GBP Manager → Products → Add product]**

**Step 1 (on screen):**
Add one entry per service you offer — not just physical products.
A tree care company would add: Tree Removal, Stump Grinding, Emergency Tree Service, Tree Pruning.
Each entry = indexed page on Google.

---

**[Screen: Typing in the product description field]**

**Step 2 (on screen):**
Write one sentence per entry.
Formula: [service] + [city or region] + [one specific detail that sets it apart].
Under 100 words. This description text gets indexed — write it in customer search language.

---

**[Screen: Uploading a real work photo to the product entry]**

**Step 3 (on screen):**
Add a real work photo to each entry.
Google shows product entries with photos more prominently in the sidebar.
No stock photos. No logos. Real work only.

---

**[Final screen: Completed product listings visible in Maps sidebar, several entries showing with photos]**

**Payoff (on screen):**
"Most clients I audit have zero product entries. Ten minutes puts you ahead of most competitors showing up in the same map pack."

CTA: "Comment below if you want the product entry template."

---
