## Topic Summaries

**Jan 12 — Carousel: The Client Pause Conversation**
Three-diagnosis framework for when a client wants to stop SEO: communication gap (show the numbers together live), timeline mismatch (month 3 always looks slow), actual underperformance (diagnose before defending). Outcome: renewed confidence or dignified exit — both better than slow drift. CTA: open comment. No flags.

**Jan 13 — Reel: GBP Completeness Score**
Five sections that drag most GBP profiles from 10/10 to 6/10: Videos (almost no one has one), Products tab (indexed text = keyword coverage), Booking link (3-min add, shows as mobile button), Q&A seeded (3–5 own FAQs), 100+ photos (ongoing activity signal). Check takes 4 minutes in any new audit. CTA: COMPLETE ⚠️. Production: screen record GBP Manager demo/blurred.

**Jan 14 — LinkedIn text + IG static: Business Address Change SEO Protocol**
Moving a business address touches every local SEO layer. Protocol: 30 days before → citation audit + batch update; day of move → GBP address update (expect re-verification, do not delete/recreate), website contact/footer/schema update; GSC no change needed. Key rule: GBP, website, schema, and all citations must match exactly. CTA: open comment. No flags.

**Jan 15 — Carousel: Franchise Local SEO**
Split-control framework: corporate controls national domain + organic rankings for broad terms; franchisee controls GBP, local reviews, local pages. Map pack runs on proximity + GBP + reviews — not corporate domain authority. Two priorities: Owner-level GBP access + consistent local reviews. Compete on "[service] in [your city]" not national terms. CTA: FRANCHISE ⚠️. New keyword — Aiden to confirm automation.

**Jan 16 — Reel: GBP Edit History as First Onboarding Check**
Before optimising a new client's GBP: check edit history (GBP Manager → Manage profile → View edit history). Reveals auto-applied third-party edits: wrong hours, category swaps, name-field changes. Affects ~1 in 3 inherited accounts. Fix via "Suggest an edit" + enable edit notifications. CTA: HISTORY (manual DM). Production: screen record with blurred business info. Distinct from Dec 16 (ongoing edit protection) — this is diagnostic use at start of new engagement.
