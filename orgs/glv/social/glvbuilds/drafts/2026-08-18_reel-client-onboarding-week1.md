## Body (3–28s)

**Day 1 (3–8s)**
GBP access confirmed.
I request manager access, not owner access — one level below, avoids any risk of account issues.
While waiting: screenshot the current GBP state as a baseline. Primary category, review count, photo count, last post date. Every metric before I touch anything.

**Day 2 (8–14s)**
Full GBP audit.
Completeness score across every section — categories, services, Q&A, photos, attributes, business description.
I use a 10-point checklist. Every gap gets logged.
Not fixing yet. Diagnosing.

**Day 3 (14–20s)**
Top keyword gaps.
`site:` operator on the client's domain to see what's indexed.
Manual map pack check for their top 5 service + city terms.
Scan their top 3 competitors' profiles for review velocity and completeness gaps.
Baseline rankings: GSC if access is available, manual check if not.

**Day 4 (20–26s)**
Baseline report set up.
GBP Performance dashboard: calls, direction requests, website clicks — screenshot every metric.
Search Console: impressions + average position for top pages.
This is the "before" snapshot. Every result in month 3 is measured against this.

**Day 5 (26–32s)**
First deliverable: a priority list.
Three quick wins, in order. Each one takes under 2 hours to implement.
Sent by end of day 5. The client sees action in the first week — even though nothing has been implemented yet.
