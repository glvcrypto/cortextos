## ARTICLE

**Title:** What GLV Marketing Does: Six Services, One Strategy, One Team

---

Most small businesses I talk to are piecing their marketing together from three or four different people.

One person for SEO. Someone else for ads. A web designer who has no idea what the SEO person is doing. An email person who's never actually seen the website.

The result is exactly what you'd expect. Everyone optimises for their own output. Nobody's responsible for whether the business is actually growing.

GLV handles six things. All of it under one roof, one strategy, one team.

### We Get You Found When People Search

Search is the one channel that builds over time. You put work in now, you see results in month four, month eight, month 24. It compounds. That's why it's usually the biggest return on the table for local businesses.

We do Google Business Profile optimisation for businesses that want to show up in the map pack and pull in local search traffic. We pair that with on-page SEO for service-area reach. So you rank locally and beyond your immediate neighbourhood.

Slow to start. Doesn't stop.

### Your Website Should Work as Hard as You Do

Your website is the hub. Every marketing channel eventually sends people there. If it's slow, confusing, or weird on mobile, every other investment takes the hit.

We build on WordPress and Vite. Clean, fast, built for search. No bloated page builders. No generic themes that look like every other small business site in your city.

Built to be maintained too. You can update your own content if you want. Or we manage it. Either way works.

### Your Email List Is the One Thing You Actually Own

Social can disappear. Ad accounts get restricted. Your email list goes nowhere.

We set up and run email for clients who want to stay in front of their customers between seasons or between purchase cycles. Welcome sequences, newsletters, reactivation campaigns for customers who've gone quiet.

The kind of email that reads like a person sent it. Not a MailChimp template.

Email compounds differently than SEO, but it compounds. A bigger engaged list means more on every single send.

### Ads That Pay for Themselves

Paid is the accelerant. Fastest way to get results. Also the fastest way to burn money without them if the foundation isn't there first.

We don't run ads until the fundamentals are solid. When they are, we run Meta and Google campaigns for clients that are ready to move faster. We write the copy, build the creative direction, manage the campaigns, and adjust based on what the data actually shows.

Not "it's performing." Why it's performing. If it isn't, we fix it.

### Want AI Working Inside Your Business?

A lot of small business owners have heard about AI and aren't sure where to start. Most of what they've been shown is demos. Stuff that looks impressive and saves zero actual time.

We set up AI integrations for businesses that want to use it for real work. Writing workflows, customer response processes, internal documentation, research. Time savings you can actually measure, not just talk about.

### AI That Never Leaves Your Building

If you're in law, medicine, finance, or another regulated industry, cloud AI is a compliance risk. Data custody, logging, PIPEDA exposure. Real concerns if you're handling sensitive client information every day.

We deploy local AI for these clients. It runs on their own server. Their data never leaves their environment. PIPEDA-ready from day one. Not a workaround. An actual solution built for regulated Canadian industries.

### We Work With Businesses That Have Something to Protect

The clients we work best with are established local or regulated businesses. Marine dealers, contractors, healthcare practices, financial services firms. Businesses with a real customer base, a real reputation, and something worth building on.

If that sounds like you, visit [glvmarketing.ca](https://glvmarketing.ca) or send a message directly.
