**Key Per-Service Pages for Electricians**

*(Supporting context — use in comments or follow-up content)*

- **Electrical panel upgrade** — "panel upgrade [city]," "electrical panel replacement [city]," "200 amp upgrade [city]" — high-value residential search; clients are usually preparing for a home addition, major appliance installation, or EV charger (the panel upgrade is often the prerequisite)
- **EV charger installation** — "EV charger installation [city]," "Level 2 charger installation [city]," "home EV charger [city]" — fastest-growing residential electrical search in Canada; rebate eligibility (federal and provincial programs — verify current program terms before publishing specific amounts); permit context (most municipalities require an ESA/Technical Safety BC inspection for Level 2 charger installs — note this, don't fabricate timelines)
- **Pot light / recessed lighting installation** — high-volume cosmetic search; homeowner renovation trigger; often bundled with basement finishing or kitchen renovation
- **Dedicated circuit installation** — kitchen appliances, home offices, hot tubs — each has its own search; "dedicated circuit for hot tub [city]" is more specific and less competitive than "electrician [city]"
- **Aluminum wiring remediation** — Ontario homes built before the mid-1970s commonly have aluminum wiring; "aluminum wiring [city]" is a safety-driven, high-intent search; clients are anxious and motivated; the trust signals on this page matter (ESA licence number, inspection process, remediation method — pigtailing vs. rewire — explained in plain language)
- **Generator hookup / transfer switch installation** — seasonal search spiking after major power outage events; "whole home generator installation [city]" and "standby generator electrician [city]" are separate intent moments
- **Commercial electrical** — tenant improvement, lighting retrofit, panel installation for commercial builds — this is a separate buyer (general contractor, property manager) with a different decision timeline; a separate commercial page with B2B trust signals (WSIB, liability coverage amount, project portfolio) captures this traffic without diluting the residential pages
