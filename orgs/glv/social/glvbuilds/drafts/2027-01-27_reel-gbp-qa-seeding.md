## Script

**Hook (on screen):**
"Most GBP Q&A sections are either empty or full of strangers' guesses. Here's how I fix that before it becomes a problem."

**Body — 3 steps, each one sentence on screen:**

Step 1: Open Google Maps in a private browser. Find your top competitor. Scroll to their Q&A section — read every question someone asked and every answer a stranger gave. That's what yours will look like if you leave it unattended.

Step 2: Write the three questions that would either qualify a good lead or prevent a wasted call for your own business. Think: "Do you service [area]?" "Do you offer [service type]?" "Are you open on [day]?"

Step 3: Ask those questions yourself from your personal Google account — then immediately switch to your GBP account and answer them accurately.

**Ending (payoff + CTA):**
"Now the first thing a potential customer sees in your Q&A is an accurate, useful answer — not a guess from two years ago.

Takes about 10 minutes. Works on any GBP profile."
