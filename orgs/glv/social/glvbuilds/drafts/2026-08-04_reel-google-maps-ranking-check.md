## Body (3–25s)

**Step 1 (3–10s)**
Open Google Maps — not Google Search.
Type the service + city directly into Maps search.
"Marine repair Sault Ste. Marie."
The order of results here is closer to what a nearby mobile searcher actually sees than the standard desktop 10-blue-links results page.

**Step 2 (10–18s)**
Note the position.
Positions 1–3 = the map pack. These get the majority of clicks.
Position 4 and below = present, but outside the featured set.
Look at who's ranked above: are they direct competitors or category-adjacent businesses?

**Step 3 (18–25s)**
Move the location pin.
In Maps, click the search bar and enter a different town in the service area.
Simulate the search from there.
What ranks #1 near the storefront address may rank #4 in a town 20km away — even within the stated service area.
That's where the ranking gap is.
