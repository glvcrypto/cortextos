## Slide 3 — Specialisation Pages

## Slide 4 — Provincial Licensing on Tech Bio Pages

AMVIC certification (Alberta), Certificate of Qualification (Ontario), Red Seal endorsement.

These go on individual technician bio pages: full name, specific certification, "more than X years in trade," real headshot.

Not buried in an About page. Google's quality raters look for this specificity on high-stakes repair queries.

## Slide 5 — Emergency Search Capture

"Emergency auto repair [city]" and "same-day oil change [city]" are high-intent and often low-competition.

One dedicated page. One GBP Q&A seed: "Do you offer emergency or same-day service?"

Defensible language in your GBP description: "including same-day appointments where availability allows" — not "always."
