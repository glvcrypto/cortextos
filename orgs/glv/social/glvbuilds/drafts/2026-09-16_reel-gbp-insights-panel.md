## LinkedIn caption

Most of my clients had been paying for local SEO for months before they ever opened the GBP Insights panel.

It's a free analytics dashboard built into every Google Business Profile. Here's what I check for every client, every month:

**1. Searches (last 28 days)**
How many times did Google show your profile? This is your baseline. If it drops, something changed — a ranking shift, a category change, or Google flagging something on your profile.

**2. Views — broken down by profile, photos, products**
Photo views especially. If your photo views are near zero, Google isn't surfacing your images in map results. That's a content signal you can act on.

**3. Direction requests**
The postcode breakdown tells you where your actual demand is coming from. If half your direction requests come from a suburb you haven't created a service page for, that's a gap.

**4. Calls — by day of week**
Which days do people click to call? Match that to when you're staffed. If you get most calls Monday but go to voicemail Monday mornings, you're burning leads Google already sent you.

**5. Website clicks**
Cross-reference this with GA4 (Source/Medium → google / gmb). If the numbers are dramatically different, there's a tracking gap.

None of this costs money. All of it informs decisions.

Comment INSIGHTS and I'll send you a direct walkthrough of where to find each one.
