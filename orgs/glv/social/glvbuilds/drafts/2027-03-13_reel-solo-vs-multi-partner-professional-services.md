## Script

**[Hook — text on screen]**
A solo lawyer and a 10-partner law firm have the same GBP listing type. Their local SEO strategy shouldn't look the same.

**[Solo — on screen]**
Solo: all reviews land on one profile. One clear primary category. Your bio page is your service page. Manageable and clean — one strong profile, consistent review collection, done.

**[Multi-partner problem 1 — on screen]**
Multi-partner: each named partner is a separate ranking opportunity.

[Name] + city + specialty. A team-grid page misses all of it. Each partner needs their own bio page.

**[Multi-partner problem 2 — on screen]**
GBP duplication risk. One firm GBP plus individual partner GBPs = you're competing with yourself in local search.

Decide who owns the firm profile before anyone creates an individual one.

**[Multi-partner problem 3 — on screen]**
Partner departures. If a departing partner is listed as GBP Primary Owner, you lose access when they leave.

The firm must always hold Primary Owner. Individuals get Manager access only.

**[Payoff — on screen]**
Solo: one strong profile, one bio page, consistent reviews.

Multi-partner: individual bio pages, firm GBP as Primary Owner, primary category aligned to your highest-revenue practice area.

**[CTA — on screen]**
What kind of practice are you working with? Solo or multi-partner — drop it in the comments.
