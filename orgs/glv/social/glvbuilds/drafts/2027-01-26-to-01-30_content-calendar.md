## New topic clusters introduced

- **Local SEO for e-commerce + physical location** (Jan 26) — Two-track framework: GBP → local pack → in-store visits; organic product pages + Merchant Center → online conversions. GA4 attribution for each channel. Revenue decides which track gets priority. Distinct from Nov 21 (Merchant Center setup) and Sep 10 (UTM tracking).
- **GBP Q&A seeding (proactive)** (Jan 27) — Competitor Q&A audit in private browser; write 3 questions that qualify/pre-empt for your own listing; ask from personal Google account, answer from GBP account. Distinct from Jul 22 (Q&A overview — what it is and why it matters).
- **Rank vs. revenue reporting** (Jan 28) — Reporting metric ordering: GBP call volume → form submissions from organic → rank as context. Shift client attention from rank tracker to conversion data. Distinct from Jun 19/Jul 20/Aug 25 (report formats) and Oct 1 (milestone report). New angle: metric philosophy and ordering.
- **Contractor/trades local SEO** (Jan 29) — Trades-specific: GBP category precision ("Plumber" not "Contractor"), service area setup for home-based trades, HomeStars for Ontario home services, licence numbers on named bio pages, emergency keyword opportunity. Distinct from Jan 19 (professional services — expertise/credentials angle) and Jan 22 (home-based business — privacy angle).
- **WordPress local SEO plugins** (Jan 30) — Practical comparison: RankMath free (local module built-in, faster for new single-location sites) vs Yoast + Local SEO add-on (multi-location schema, worth it if already on Yoast). What neither handles: auto-linking multiple GBP profiles to individual location pages. Distinct from Jul 21 (LocalBusiness schema generally) and Dec 18 (CMS choice broadly).
