# 2027-02-27 | LinkedIn + Instagram | Reel (~30–35s) | CTA: open comment

**Date:** February 27, 2027
**Platform:** LinkedIn + Instagram
**Content type:** Reel — ~30–35 seconds
**CTA:** Open comment (no automation needed)
**Topic:** GBP posts when you "have nothing to promote" — three content formats that always work
**MEMORY cluster:** GBP post content ideas — no-promotion formats (new — add after session; distinct from Sep 30 post batching strategy workflow and Aug 19 offer post format)

---

## Script

**[Screen: GBP Manager → Posts tab showing a profile with zero posts or the last post from months ago]**

**Hook (on screen + voiceover):**
"Every client says the same thing when I ask about their GBP posts: 'I have nothing to post about this week.' Here's how I handle it."

---

**[Screen: GBP Manager → New What's New post being typed]**

**Format 1 (on screen):**
**Seasonal tip.**
Write the one question your customers ask most this time of year.
Answer it in one sentence.
Example: "Spring HVAC check — replace the filter before you run the cooling for the first time. Extends the system life."
Relevant today. Takes 60 seconds to write.

---

**[Screen: GBP Manager → Q&A tab showing a question, then switching to New Post to write a What's New from the same answer]**

**Format 2 (on screen):**
**FAQ post from your Q&A section.**
Open your GBP Q&A tab. Pick one question.
Copy the answer into a What's New post.
Seeds the Q&A and keeps the post calendar filled — same work, two outputs.

---

**[Screen: A real behind-the-scenes photo being added to a GBP post — team member at work, no client faces visible]**

**Format 3 (on screen):**
**Behind-the-scenes photo post.**
One real photo of your team or workspace.
One sentence about what's happening in it.
No graphics, no stock images.
Google factors active posting into profile signals. A photo post counts.

---

**[Final screen: GBP Manager → Posts showing three posts from the last 30 days, one of each format]**

**Payoff (on screen):**
"One GBP post per week. You don't need a promotion — you need consistency.
Seasonal tip. FAQ repurpose. Real photo. Pick one."

CTA: "Comment what format you'll try first."

---

## Production Notes

- Use a demo GBP account for screen recording — no real client data visible
- For Format 3, source a real work photo from the GLV or Reyco library if needed, or use a clearly illustrative placeholder — blur any client-identifying details
- The GBP Q&A → post crossover in Format 2 is the strongest tip for most businesses — give it the most screen time
- Keep each format segment to about 7–8 seconds
- No music needed — clean voiceover only
