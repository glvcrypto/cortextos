# GBP Post Batching Strategy — Reel (~35s)

**Hook (0–5s)**
> "I write and schedule all my clients' GBP posts for the month in 15 minutes. Here's the system."

**Body (5–28s)**

[Screen: GBP Manager → Updates → Create post]

Step 1 — Once a month, open a blank doc and write four posts: one What's New, one Offer (if you have one running), one seasonal tip relevant to that month, one FAQ pulled from your GBP Q&A.

Step 2 — Keep each post under 300 characters for mobile readability. Add a UTM-tagged link to your most relevant service page on each one.

Step 3 — Schedule them in GBP Manager — one per week, spread evenly across the month.

Step 4 — Duplicate the doc at the end of the month. Update the topics and the service page link. That's your template for next month.

**Payoff (28–35s)**
> "15 minutes once a month. Posts stay current. GBP sees consistent activity. Most new clients I work with have nothing scheduled when I find them."
