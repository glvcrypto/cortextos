**Clear your GSC Coverage error list.**

GSC → Index → Pages → Error tab.

Dec 31 and Jan 1 are statutory holidays. Set the correct special hours in GBP Manager before December 25 — changes take a few days to propagate.

Three minutes. Almost no one does this until the first confused call comes in on New Year's Eve.
