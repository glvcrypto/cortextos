# 2027-02-17 | LinkedIn + Instagram | Reel (~30–35s) | CTA: open comment

**Date:** February 17, 2027
**Platform:** LinkedIn + Instagram
**Content type:** Reel — ~30–35 seconds
**CTA:** Open comment (no automation needed)
**Topic:** Responding to positive Google reviews — the missed conversion opportunity
**MEMORY cluster:** Review generation / response (distinct from Aug 7 negative response formula)

---

## Script

**[Screen: GBP Manager → Reviews tab showing several 5-star reviews with no owner responses]**

**Hook (on screen + voiceover):**
"Most businesses respond to negative reviews and ignore the positive ones. Here's why that's a conversion gap."

---

**[Screen: Google Maps listing — someone scrolling through reviews, including owner responses]**

**Step 1 (on screen):**
People deciding whether to call read owner responses — on positive reviews too.
A review with no response signals an owner who isn't paying attention.

---

**[Screen: GBP Manager — typing a response to a 5-star review]**

**Step 2 (on screen):**
Two-sentence formula:
1. Thank them and name the specific service they mentioned.
2. One line: invite them back or note you're there when they need you next.

Example: "Thank you, Sarah — glad the furnace tune-up went smoothly before the cold hit. We're here whenever you need us."

---

**[Screen: GBP Manager → Settings → Notifications → Reviews toggle ON]**

**Step 3 (on screen):**
Enable GBP review notifications in the app.
Respond within 48 hours of the review posting.
Leads that check reviews are often ready to call — your response is the last thing they read.

---

**[Final screen: Side-by-side — 5-star reviews with no response vs. 5-star reviews with owner responses]**

**Payoff (on screen):**
"Your responses are marketing copy every future customer reads before calling."

CTA: "Drop a comment if you want the 2-sentence response template."

---
