## Slide 2 — Tool 1: Google Maps (private browser)

**Tool 1: Google Maps — private browser**

Search the client's primary keyword in a private window.

What I note:
- Which competitor ranks #1 in the map pack?
- How many reviews do they have vs. my client?
- What primary category are they using?

---

## Slide 3 — Tool 2: Competitor's GBP profile

**Tool 2: Open the #1 competitor's GBP profile directly**

What I check:
- Services listed (are there gaps my client fills but doesn't mention?)
- Photos: how many, which categories, how recent?
- Q&A section: what are customers asking?
- Posts: are they posting? How often?

---

## Slide 4 — Tool 3: Google Search (brand name)

**Tool 3: Search the competitor's brand name**

What I look for:
- Do they rank for their own name cleanly? (If not, weak brand authority)
- What pages Google indexes for them — service pages, location pages, blog posts?
- Any review site results in top 10 (Yelp, HomeStars, BBB)?

---

## Slide 5 — The gap scorecard

**After 3 tools, I score 4 gaps:**

1. Review gap — how far behind is my client?
2. Category gap — is the competitor using a more specific primary category?
3. Content gap — do they have service/location pages my client doesn't?
4. Activity gap — are they posting on GBP when my client isn't?

---

## Slide 6 — What I do with it

**The gaps become the 90-day plan:**

- Review gap → review generation system, week 1
- Category gap → research + change primary category, first session
- Content gap → page brief + publish timeline
- Activity gap → GBP post calendar (2–4/month minimum)
