## Script

**Hook (on screen):**
"There are pages on your site that are almost ranking. GSC shows exactly which ones. Here's the 5-minute check."

**Body — 3 steps, each one sentence on screen:**

Step 1: Go to GSC → Performance → Pages. Click "Average position" to sort. Look for pages sitting between position 11 and 20 with at least 50 impressions in the last 28 days — that's a page Google is testing just outside the first page.

Step 2: Click the page, then switch to the Queries tab. Find the service+city query it's showing for. Check whether that query appears in the page title, H1, and first paragraph — if not, add it.

Step 3: Find one stronger page on the same site — a homepage, a service hub, or a well-ranking page — and add an internal link from there to the underperforming page using the target query as anchor text.

**Ending (payoff + CTA):**
"Two targeted changes — title tag and one internal link — can move a page from position 14 to position 6 faster than building a brand new page ever will.

Pages you already have are often the fastest wins in local SEO."
