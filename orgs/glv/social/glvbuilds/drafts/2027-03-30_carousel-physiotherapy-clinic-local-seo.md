## Slide 2 — GBP Category Precision

Google has distinct categories: Physiotherapist, Occupational Therapist, Sports Medicine Clinic, Rehabilitation Centre, Physical Therapy Clinic.

"Physiotherapist" doesn't rank for sports medicine searches. "Rehabilitation Centre" doesn't rank for OT-specific queries.

Check what your top 3 map pack competitors are using as their primary category. Set yours to match your highest-search-intent service — then add relevant secondaries.

---

## Slide 3 — Individual Practitioner Bio Pages

Each physiotherapist in your clinic has their own search demand: name + city + specialty.

A dedicated bio page — full name, PT or OT designation, provincial college registration number (CPO in Ontario, CPTBC in BC, CPTA in Alberta), specific areas of focus, real headshot — creates one ranking opportunity per clinician.

One team-grid page is one opportunity where there could be six.

---

## Slide 4 — Referral Process FAQ

"Do I need a doctor's referral for physiotherapy?" is one of the most searched pre-appointment questions in this vertical.

If you offer direct access (no referral required), that is a conversion-critical answer that belongs on your homepage and in your GBP Q&A — not buried in your FAQ page footer.

Seed it in GBP Q&A from your personal Google account, then answer it from your GBP Manager account the same session.

---

## Slide 5 — PHIPA-Compliant Review Asks

You cannot acknowledge a patient relationship in writing. That rules out most standard review request scripts.

Compliant framing: "If you had a positive experience at our clinic, a Google review helps other patients find the right care. Mention the type of treatment if you're comfortable."

Reviews naming specific services or therapists appear as justification text in local pack results. The prompt matters.

---

## Slide 6 — Service-Specific Pages

"Physiotherapy [city]" is the generic term. "Sports injury physio [city]," "post-surgical rehab [city]," and "pelvic floor physiotherapy [city]" are lower-competition searches with patients who know exactly what they need.

Build one dedicated page per highest-revenue service type. Start with the service that drives the most new patient inquiries, not the one you rank for already.
