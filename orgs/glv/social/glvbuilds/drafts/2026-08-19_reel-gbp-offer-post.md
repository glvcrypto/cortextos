# GBP Posts That Actually Drive Calls — One Format Does Most of the Work

## Hook (on screen + voice-over)

I post to my clients' Google Business Profiles every week.
One post type consistently generates calls.
The rest just look like activity.

---

## Body (on screen, one line per beat)

**What doesn't generate calls:**
- "Thank you for choosing us!" → no one searches for this
- Generic photo with no caption → no keyword context, no action
- Event post with no urgency → scrolled past

**What does generate calls: the offer post**

Step 1 — Name the service exactly (match the words customers search)
Step 2 — One line on what's included or who it's for
Step 3 — A real deadline ("Book before August 31" or "Limited spots this week")
Step 4 — Link to your service page or booking form

**Why it works:**
Offer posts get a dedicated card in the GBP panel.
The deadline creates action without fake pressure.
The keyword in the post title helps Google match it to searches.

---

Stop posting to look active.
Post to get the phone to ring.
