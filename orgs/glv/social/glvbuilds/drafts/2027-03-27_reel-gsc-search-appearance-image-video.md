## Script

**[Hook — text on screen]**
There's a filter in GSC that most local business owners have never touched.

It shows you traffic from Google Images and YouTube video search — separately from your main web traffic.

**[Step 1 — on screen]**
GSC → Performance. See the "Search type" dropdown at the top? It defaults to "Web."

Switch it to "Image." You're now looking at impressions and clicks coming from Google Image search only.

Switch it to "Video." If you have YouTube content embedded on indexed pages, this shows search impressions from YouTube.

**[Step 2 — on screen]**
What Image search shows for local businesses: service photos, before/after shots, infographics on blog posts.

Click "Queries" to see what people searched before clicking your image.

High impressions, low clicks = the image is appearing but not getting chosen. Fix: descriptive filename (not "IMG_4821.jpg"), specific alt text matching the query.

**[Step 3 — on screen]**
What to do with the data:

Image: if an image is getting impressions for a query you're not ranking for in Web search, check the page it's on and strengthen that page.

Video: if you have YouTube content but zero Video search impressions, check whether the video is embedded on a public, crawlable page — not just on YouTube.

**[Payoff — on screen]**
Most local businesses check Web search only.

This takes 90 seconds. It's additional data on traffic you're already getting — just not seeing.

**[CTA — on screen]**
Check your Image search tab this week.
