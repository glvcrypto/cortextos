# GBP Completeness Score — The 5 Sections Most Businesses Miss

## Hook (on screen)

"Most GBP profiles score 6 out of 10. Here are the five sections dragging the score down."

---

## Body (3–4 steps, one sentence each on screen)

**Step 1 — Videos.** Almost no client has one. A 30-second clip of real work shows on Maps before the website click-through.

**Step 2 — Products tab.** Works for service businesses. Indexed text means extra keyword coverage Google sees in the profile.

**Step 3 — Booking link.** Three-minute add in Edit profile → Booking. Shows as a button on mobile listings before visitors reach the website.

**Step 4 — Q&A seeded.** Answer 3–5 of your own frequently asked questions before customers ask bad ones. These appear directly on the listing.

**Step 5 — 100+ photos.** Most businesses stop at 10–15 and never add more. Ongoing photo activity signals an active, live business to Google.
