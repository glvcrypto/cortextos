## Body (3–28s)

**Step 1 (3–11s)**
Open your GBP dashboard.
Go to Messages.
If it says "Turn on messaging" — it's off.
That means every potential customer who taps the chat icon on your Google profile gets a dead end.
The message button still appears in your knowledge panel — they just can't reach you.

**Step 2 (11–19s)**
Turn it on.
Once enabled, write a welcome message.
Make it useful: "Hi — thanks for reaching out. What service do you need and what's your equipment make and model? We'll get back to you within [timeframe]."
That one question qualifies the lead before you've spent a single minute on the phone.

**Step 3 (19–28s)**
Set a response time expectation.
GBP shows your average response time publicly on your profile.
A business that responds in under an hour displays that on the listing.
Respond within business hours. Don't let messages sit for days — Google can turn off messaging for businesses with very slow response rates.

## LinkedIn Caption

A lot of local businesses have a chat button on their Google Business Profile.

A lot of them have never turned messaging on.

The button still appears in the knowledge panel. Potential customers click it. They get a message that the business isn't available. They move to the next result.

Here's the setup I walk every new client through.

**Step 1: Check if messaging is on.**
Open your GBP dashboard → Messages. If it says "Turn on messaging," it's off. Enable it. Takes about 30 seconds.

**Step 2: Write a welcome message that qualifies the lead.**
The welcome message goes out automatically when someone starts a chat.
Don't write "Thanks for contacting us!" and leave it there.
Write: "Hi — thanks for reaching out. To point you to the right person, can you tell us what service you need and your equipment make and model? We'll reply within [timeframe]."
That question does triage before anyone on your team picks up the thread. You know what service they need, what equipment they have, and roughly how complex the job is — before the first response.

**Step 3: Respond within business hours.**
GBP displays your average response time publicly on your profile. A business that replies in under an hour gets that shown on their listing.
Respond during business hours. Don't let messages age for multiple days — Google monitors response rates and can disable messaging for businesses with consistently slow replies.

Three steps. About 5 minutes to set up. Most competitors haven't done it.
