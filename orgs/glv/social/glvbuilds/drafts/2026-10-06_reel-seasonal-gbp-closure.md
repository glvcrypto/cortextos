# Seasonal GBP Closure — Reel (~35s)

**Hook (0–5s)**
> "My client closes every winter. Here's what I do in GBP before their last day of the season."

**Body (5–28s)**

[Screen: GBP Manager — Business Information / Special Hours / Mark as temporarily closed]

Step 1 — Mark as Temporarily Closed in GBP Manager. Don't delete the listing. This keeps reviews, photos, and ranking signals intact while showing the correct status to anyone who searches.

Step 2 — Set Special Hours for every closed month. Don't leave regular hours showing during the closure — Google will flag the listing as "may be closed" on holidays regardless.

Step 3 — Update the GBP description to include seasonal context. "Open April through October." One sentence is enough.

Step 4 — Add a Q&A entry: "Are you open in winter?" Answer with the reopening month. Seed it yourself through Google Maps.

Step 5 — Draft a "We're back for the season" GBP What's New post right now, and schedule it for two weeks before your spring reopening. Write it today so you don't forget in March.
