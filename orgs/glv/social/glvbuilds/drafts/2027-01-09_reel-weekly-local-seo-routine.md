# Reel: My 10-minute weekly local SEO check — every client, every week

## Script

**Hook (on screen + voiceover):**
"Five things I check for every local SEO client every week. Ten minutes. Catches most problems before they compound."

**Body:**

[Beat 1 — on screen: "1. GBP Insights — 2 min"]
"Views and calls compared to last week. Any unusual drop tells me something changed — an algorithm shift, a bad review, or an auto-applied edit. I investigate before the client notices."

[Beat 2 — on screen: "2. New reviews — 1 min"]
"Any negatives? I respond that day, not that week. A public response within 24 hours matters."

[Beat 3 — on screen: "3. GBP suggested edits — 1 min"]
"GBP Manager shows any third-party edit suggestions. Google sometimes auto-applies them. Catching a NAP change here before it propagates saves hours later."

[Beat 4 — on screen: "4. GSC impressions — 3 min"]
"Top 3 service pages, week-over-week. Stable is fine. A sudden drop means an indexation issue or a penalty — fix it immediately, not next week."

[Beat 5 — on screen: "5. One action item — 2 min"]
"Write down the one highest-priority task for this client before closing the tabs. It's the first task of next week's session."

**Ending (on screen: "Ten minutes. Five checks. Almost nothing slips through."):**
"This routine catches the things that don't show up in monthly reports until they've already compounded."

**CTA (on screen):**
Comment **WEEKLY** and I'll send you the five-check template I use.
