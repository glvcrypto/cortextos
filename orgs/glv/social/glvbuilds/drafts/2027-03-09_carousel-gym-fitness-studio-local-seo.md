## LinkedIn Caption

Most gym owners I talk to are optimising for "gym near me." That keyword exists. But it's not how most people find a specific fitness studio.

They search for what they want to do. Prenatal yoga. Beginner boxing. Kids jiu-jitsu. Evening spin class. The map pack serves up whoever appears for that specific search — and most studios are set up for the generic version.

Here's what changes when you treat a fitness studio like a multi-service local business:

**GBP category precision**
Google has distinct categories: Yoga Studio, Pilates Studio, Martial Arts School, CrossFit Gym, Dance Studio, Boxing Gym. "Fitness Centre" or "Gym" doesn't rank for filter searches. Check the primary category of the top 3 competitors in your city. Set yours to your actual model.

**Instructor bio pages**
Each instructor has their own search demand — name + city + specialty. A page per instructor (full name, certifications, what they teach, real headshot, class schedule or booking link) lets each rank independently. Most studios have one team-grid page. That's one ranking opportunity where there could be six.

**GBP booking link**
GBP → Edit profile → Booking. Add your Mindbody, WellnessLiving, or ClassPass URL. It displays as a "Book a class" button on mobile before the website click-through. Takes 3 minutes. Almost never set on studio audits.

**Review specificity**
Reviews that name the instructor and class type appear as justification text in local pack results — snippets beneath your listing that show exactly what Google pulls from your reviews. When asking, prompt: "mention the class or instructor if you're comfortable."

**Service pages per class format**
"Yoga near me" is competitive. "Prenatal yoga [city]" usually isn't. Add one page per format you offer — start with your highest-revenue format. 300–400 words, your instructor's name, one practical detail about the class, a booking CTA.

Most of this takes less time than one redesign project. It just requires treating each format and instructor as a separate local SEO asset.
