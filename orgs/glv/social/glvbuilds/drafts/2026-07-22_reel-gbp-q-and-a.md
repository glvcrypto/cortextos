## LinkedIn Caption

Most local business owners I talk to have never touched the Q&A section on their Google Business Profile.

It's sitting there, blank, in their knowledge panel — visible to every potential customer who searches for them.

Here's what I do for every new local SEO client:

**1. Seed 3–5 questions yourself.**
Don't wait for customers. Post the questions they actually ask — "Do you offer same-day service?", "Do you work on [equipment type]?", "Is there parking?" Use the exact phrasing a searcher would type.

**2. Answer immediately, with keywords.**
"Yes, we offer same-day marine service in Sault Ste. Marie for most outboard motors." That's not stuffing — that's a complete, useful answer that happens to include the terms that matter.

**3. Reply to every customer question that comes in.**
Google weights Q&A activity. Unanswered questions sit there as signals of low engagement.

The Q&A section is one of the most ignored parts of a GBP — which means seeding it is a low-competition win most competitors skip.

10 minutes of setup. Visible in the knowledge panel for every search that triggers your profile.
