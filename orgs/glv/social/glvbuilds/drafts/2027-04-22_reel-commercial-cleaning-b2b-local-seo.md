## Reel Script

**Hook (on screen):**
"Every commercial cleaning company I audit ranks for their company name. Almost none rank for 'office cleaning services [city].'"

---

**Beat 1 — Search by building type:**
"'Office cleaning services [city].' 'Medical office cleaning [city].' 'School janitorial services [city].' 'Restaurant kitchen cleaning [city].' These are separate searches made by different buyers with completely different requirements. One 'commercial cleaning services' page ranks for none of them specifically. One page per building type — starting with the contract value you're trying to grow."

---

**Beat 2 — GBP category:**
"'Janitorial Service' and 'Commercial Cleaning Service' are both valid primary GBP categories for commercial cleaning. 'House Cleaning Service' is not — that's a residential category. Using the wrong primary category means you're appearing in filters used by residential buyers who can't hire you, and missing the filters used by facility managers who can."

---

**Beat 3 — B2B conversion signals:**
"Commercial cleaning buyers aren't booking on a contact form. They're checking: are you insured, do you have WSIB or WCB clearance, are you ISSA Canada certified, and is there a named account manager who answers the phone? Those four things — visible on your website and in your GBP description — are what separate a shortlisted vendor from a company a facility manager has never heard of."

---

**Payoff + CTA (on screen):**
"Building-type pages. Correct GBP category. WSIB/WCB visible. Named contact above the fold."
