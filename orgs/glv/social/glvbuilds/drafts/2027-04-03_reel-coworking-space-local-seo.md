## Reel Script

"'Day pass office [city]' is the most urgent search in this category. That person needs a desk today. Not next week. If your phone number and day pass option aren't visible in the first five seconds of landing on the page, they've already moved on."

---

**Beat 3 — GBP attributes as filters:**
"Google Maps has filter options specific to working spaces: 24/7 access, meeting rooms, parking, Wi-Fi. If those attributes aren't checked in your GBP, you're invisible when someone filters by them. Takes five minutes in Edit profile → More."

---

**Payoff + CTA (on screen):**
"Day pass. Hot desk. Private office. Each intent needs its own page. The attributes take five minutes."
