## ARTICLE

**Title:** What GLV Marketing Does: Six Services, One Strategy, One Team

---

### We Do Six Things. We Do Them in One Place.

Most small businesses piece together their marketing from multiple vendors. One person for SEO. A different freelancer for ads. Another for email. A web designer who isn't connected to any of the above.

That approach has a built-in problem: no one is responsible for the strategy that ties it all together. Each vendor optimises for their own output, not for what your business actually needs.

GLV handles six core services under one roof, with one strategy running across all of them. Here's what that looks like.

---

### Search Engine Optimisation and Local SEO

Search is where most small business marketing either compounds or collapses. Done well, it drives organic traffic that keeps growing over time. Done poorly, it produces reports that look impressive but don't move the needle.

We focus on Google Business Profile optimisation for local businesses that want to show up in the map pack and attract local search traffic. We pair that with on-page SEO for service-area reach, so businesses rank beyond just their immediate neighbourhood.

For most of our clients, local SEO is where they see the biggest return over time. It's the most durable of the six services we offer.

---

### Website Design and Development

Your website is the hub. Every marketing channel points to it eventually. If it's slow, unclear, or difficult to navigate on a phone, every other marketing investment suffers for it.

We design and build on WordPress and Vite. Clean, fast, built for search performance. No bloated page builders, no generic themes. A website that reflects the quality of the business and does the actual job of converting visitors.

The sites we build are also designed to be maintained. If you want to update your own content, you can. If you want us to manage it, we do that too.

---

### Email Marketing

Email is the one channel you actually own. Social accounts can be suspended. Ad accounts can be restricted. Your email list goes nowhere.

We set up and run email marketing for clients who want to stay in front of their customers between seasons or between purchase cycles. That includes welcome automations, regular newsletters, and reactivation campaigns designed to bring back customers who've gone quiet.

Email compounds differently than SEO, but it compounds. The bigger and more engaged the list, the more leverage every send has.

---

### Paid Advertising

Paid ads are the accelerant. They produce results faster than organic channels, but they require a foundation to perform well. We don't run ads until the fundamentals are in place, because that's the fastest way to spend money without getting results.

When the foundation is solid, we run Meta and Google campaigns for businesses that are ready to grow faster. We write the copy, build the creative direction, manage the campaigns, and optimise based on what the data actually shows.

We don't hide behind vague performance metrics. If it's working, we show you why. If it isn't, we adjust.

---

### AI Integration

A lot of small businesses have heard about AI but aren't sure where to start. Most of the use cases they've been shown are demos, not real workflow improvements.

We set up AI integrations for small and mid-size businesses that want to use AI for actual work: writing processes, customer response workflows, internal documentation, research pipelines. The goal is always meaningful time savings on real tasks, not novelty for its own sake.

---

### Local AI for Regulated Industries

For businesses in law, medicine, finance, and other regulated industries, cloud-based AI tools carry compliance risk. When sensitive client data runs through third-party servers, there are questions about custody, logging, and regulatory exposure under PIPEDA.

We deploy local AI systems for these clients. The AI runs on their own servers, their data never leaves their environment, and the capability is built specifically for their industry. PIPEDA-ready from day one.

This is an increasingly relevant service line for Northern Ontario businesses in regulated sectors. The capability is real and the setup is more accessible than most people expect.

---

### We Work With Businesses That Have Something to Protect

The clients we work best with are established local or regulated businesses: marine dealers, contractors, healthcare practices, financial services firms. Businesses with a real customer base, a real reputation, and something worth compounding.

If that sounds like your business and you want marketing that stacks instead of resets, visit [glvmarketing.ca](https://glvmarketing.ca) or send a message directly.
