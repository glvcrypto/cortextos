# Reel: "Why am I not number 1 yet?" — how I answer this

## Script

**Hook (on screen + voiceover):**
"Every local SEO client asks this question. Here's exactly how I answer it."

**Body:**

[Beat 1 — on screen: "First: clarify what 'number 1' means."]
"The map pack? Organic results? Your brand name or a service keyword? They're different ranking systems. Most clients mean all three at once — and that matters when you're explaining progress."

[Beat 2 — on screen: "Then show the trend, not the position."]
"I pull GBP Insights — views and calls month over month. I pull GSC impressions for the service pages. Position fluctuates. An upward trend in impressions and calls is what we're building toward."

[Beat 3 — on screen: "Then reset the timeline expectation."]
"Local SEO compounds. Month 3 looks slow. Month 9 looks different. I show them the upward trend and remind them we're building something that keeps working after we stop pushing."

**Ending (on screen: "The question isn't wrong. The frame just needs adjusting."):**
"They're asking whether it's working. That's a fair question. Show them the data that actually answers it."
