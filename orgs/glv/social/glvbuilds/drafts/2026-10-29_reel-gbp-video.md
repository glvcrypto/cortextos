## LinkedIn caption

Google Business Profile lets you add a short video directly to your listing. It appears in your profile on Google Maps — before a visitor clicks through to your website.

I check this in every new client audit. Almost nobody has it set up.

**The specs:**

- Under 30 seconds
- Under 75MB
- Minimum 720p resolution
- Portrait (9:16) or landscape — both render in the profile

**Where to upload:**

GBP Manager → Photos → Videos. Google moderates new uploads — expect 24 hours before it goes live.

**What to film:**

Real-location content performs better than polished stock video. Your team at work. A walk through the shop or service area. A before-and-after. The goal is to show what your business actually looks like — that's the trust signal.

**Why it matters:**

A video in your GBP profile adds an engagement layer before anyone has visited your website. It's indexed, it displays in search, and in most categories, your competitors don't have one.
