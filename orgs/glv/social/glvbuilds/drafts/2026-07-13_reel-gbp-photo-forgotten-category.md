## LinkedIn Description

The GBP photo category most local businesses never touch.

At-work photos.

GBP has a dedicated "At Work" category in the photo upload flow. Most clients upload an exterior shot and stop there.

At-work photos — a tech doing a repair, a staff member helping a customer — get higher click engagement than building photos. Google tracks that engagement. It's a local pack signal.

The move I recommend to every client: take 5–10 at-work shots per month on a phone. Upload them. GBP rewards fresh photo activity, not a one-time batch.

Photo baseline I check on every audit:
- 3+ exterior
- 3+ interior
- 5+ at-work (refreshed monthly)
