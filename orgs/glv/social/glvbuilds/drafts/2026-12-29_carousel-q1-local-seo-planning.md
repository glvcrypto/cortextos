# Carousel: How I plan the first 90 days for every local SEO client

## Slide 1 — Hook

"January 1 is not when I start planning Q1 for my clients.

I do it the last week of December. Same 6 steps, every account."

## Slide 2 — Step 1: Check Q4 rank movement

GSC Performance → compare Oct–Dec vs. Jul–Sep for every service page.

- Pages that gained: protect them — internal link tighten, GBP post on the service, schema recheck
- Pages that lost: add to Q1 refresh list — don't rewrite yet, diagnose first (Coverage tab, cannibalisation check)

The list takes 15 minutes to build. It's 80% of the January plan.

## Slide 3 — Step 2: Confirm GBP info is still accurate

Annual sweep before December ends:

- Business name, phone, URL, address format
- Primary category (are top competitors still using the same one?)
- GBP description — any business changes the client hasn't flagged yet?
- Edit history — has Google auto-applied a third-party suggestion this quarter?

Takes 10 minutes. Catches problems before they compound into January.

## Slide 4 — Step 3: Set 3 specific rank targets

Pick 3 service+city queries that matter most to this client.

Write down current GSC position + GBP Insights monthly search count.

These are the 90-day success metrics. Not traffic. Not impressions. Three specific queries.

Put them at the top of the Q1 plan document. Revisit at 30, 60, 90 days.

## Slide 5 — Step 4: Plan the first month of GBP posts

Four types. Write them now, schedule for January:

1. What's New: one Q4 win framed as a result ("Now accepting spring bookings")
2. Offer: if applicable — deadline-anchored, UTM-tagged link
3. Seasonal tip: relevant to the client's category in January
4. FAQ from Q&A: pull a real customer question, answer it in <300 chars

This takes 20 minutes. Consistency across 12 months matters more than any single post.

## Slide 6 — Step 5: Book the Q1 check-in calls

Three touchpoints, scheduled now:

- 30-day (15 min): what happened in January? Any surprises?
- 60-day (30 min): rank movement + page adjustments needed
- 90-day (45 min): milestone report + next quarter goals

These almost never happen if they're not in the calendar before December ends.

## Slide 7 — Step 6: Add one new conversion metric

Each quarter, the report should get one data point richer.

If Q3/Q4 only tracked traffic + rank: add GBP call count or form submissions in Q1.
If calls were already tracked: ask the client for close rate monthly — even an estimate.

By month 12, you have a one-page report that tells the actual business story.
