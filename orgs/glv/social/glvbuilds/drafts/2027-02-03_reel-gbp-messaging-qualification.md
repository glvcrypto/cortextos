## Script

**Hook (on screen):**
"Most GBP messaging inboxes are turned on — and then completely ignored. Here's the welcome message I write for every service area client."

**Body — 3 steps, each one sentence on screen:**

Step 1: Turn on messaging in GBP Manager → Messages → Turn on. Set a response time you can actually commit to — "within a few hours" only if that's true.

Step 2: Write a welcome message that pre-qualifies the lead. Two questions: what service do you need, and what city or postal code are you in? This filters unserviceable requests before they become wasted calls.

Step 3: Enable notifications on your phone so you see incoming messages the same day. A lead that waits 48 hours for a reply has already called someone else.

**Ending (payoff + CTA):**
"The welcome message does two jobs: it signals that you're responsive — which Google factors into messaging rank — and it gives you the information you need to reply usefully on the first message.
