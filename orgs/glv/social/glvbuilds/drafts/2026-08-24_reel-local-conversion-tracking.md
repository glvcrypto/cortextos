## Body (on screen, one line per beat)

**Number 1 — GBP Calls**
Google Business Profile → Performance tab → Phone calls
Shows how many people clicked your phone number from search.
This is your primary lead signal.

**Number 2 — Direction Requests**
GBP Performance → Directions
Shows how many people asked Google for directions to your location.
High numbers = local searchers actively considering you.

**Number 3 — Website Clicks from GBP**
GBP Performance → Website clicks
Shows traffic driven from your Business Profile.
Compare month-over-month — not just the number, the trend.

**Number 4 — Service Page Traffic**
Google Search Console → Pages tab → filter to your service pages
Shows organic clicks from people searching for what you offer.
