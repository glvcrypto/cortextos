### Logo Usage Rules

- **GLV Marketing** = full brand name (primary logo)
- **GLV** = icon/mark only (square contexts)
- Never use "Digital Marketing Services" in the logo (descriptive copy, not brand mark)
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "L"
- Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 10mm for print

## Colours

### Primary Palette

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| **Black** | #000000 | Primary text, logo |
| **White** | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, reversed logo |
| **Deep Red** | #B22222 | Accent colour: highlights, dividers, tags, CTAs |

### Usage Rules

- Black and white are the foundation. Every design should work in pure monochrome first.
- Deep red is the accent, not the base. Use it sparingly for visual punch: accent lines, pill badges, underlines, icon highlights, CTA buttons.
- Never use red for body text or large background fills.
- Typical ratio: 70% white, 25% black, 5% red.
- The red comes from the logo set (variants 10-15) and ties the full brand together.
