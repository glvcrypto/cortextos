```

A growing number of people are not typing keywords into Google the way they used to. They are asking AI tools full questions and getting direct answers. Google's AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity. This is where Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) comes in.

GEO is the practice of making sure your business shows up in those AI-generated answers. It is different from traditional SEO. It is not just about ranking on a results page. It is about being the business that AI recommends when someone asks "Who is the best [your service] near me?"

For local businesses, this matters more than most people realize. If someone asks an AI tool for a recommendation in your area and your competitor shows up but you do not, that is a lost customer you never even knew about.

The good news: the businesses that start optimizing for AI search now will have a significant head start. Most of your local competitors have not even heard of GEO yet.

At GLV Marketing, we build GEO into every client strategy alongside traditional SEO. Because showing up on Google is still important. But showing up in AI answers is where things are headed.

Want to know how your business currently appears in AI search results? Send me a message.
```
