## How to read this list

- **Membership 500+** is the GLV floor for "worth scout's monitoring time." Smaller groups can have value but yield fewer signals per scan cycle.
- **Post frequency** is bucketed: daily / 3-5x weekly / weekly / sub-weekly.
- **Aiden membership status** is flagged as **VERIFY** for every entry. Scout cannot inspect Aiden's personal FB account membership state from outside the session; he must confirm yes/no per group on his next pass.
- **Member counts** marked `unverified:` are scout-inference from group visibility and SSM-population proxy. Direct counts require an authenticated FB session, which scout does not run. Aiden's first-pass through this list will yield exact numbers; flag for refresh after that.
- **Relevance tier:** P1 = monitor + engage actively, P2 = monitor + opportunistic engage, P3 = monitor-only, low-signal.
- **Intent signals** are the post-types that justify scout's scanning cycle: leads (someone asking for the service GLV sells), competitors (other agencies posting), client-segment activity (small biz owners venting/asking), event triggers (community moments GLV can reference).
