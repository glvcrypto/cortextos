- ESA licence #7000464. TSSA licensed. Fire alarms, security, solar, infrared scanning, data cabling.
- Zero Google reviews across all platforms. Not on ThreeBestRated SSM list. Tombari Electric — founded 2015, 38 years younger — has 23 Google reviews and outranks them on every platform.
- Italian language listed on directories. Family-owned. Anthony (founder) + Victor (MBA, Operations Manager) — succession in progress.
- No current agency relationship detected.

**The pitch:**
Victor has an MBA. He knows what marketing ROI looks like. Lead with the credibility gap — they've done more prestigious work than any competitor in SSM, but have zero proof online. Homeowners, property managers, and commercial GCs searching today have no way to find them. The ask is straightforward: get their reputation online to match the work they've already done.

**Watch for:**
- "We've never needed it" — respond with: "You didn't need it when you were the only game in town. Tombari has been spending PPC budget for 3 years to take the work you're not visible for."
- Anthony may need to be in the room for budget approval. Don't close with Victor alone if Anthony is available.

**Month 1 plan to reference:**
1. Claim + rebuild GBP. Even 8–10 reviews puts them in the top 3 local search results.
2. Surface the project portfolio on the website — nursing home and border crossing are buried right now.
