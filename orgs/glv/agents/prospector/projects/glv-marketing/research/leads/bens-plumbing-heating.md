## Red Flags

### 1. Dead Website Domain
The domain bensplumbingandheating.ca does not resolve at all. This is not a hosting issue -- DNS itself is gone. The domain may be expired. If someone else registers it, they could impersonate the business or redirect traffic. This needs to be checked and secured immediately.

### 2. Multiple Addresses -- Is the Business Operational?
Three different addresses across directories raises the question: has Ben moved locations, or is the business operating from multiple/changing sites? The GBP address (1745 Seymour St) differs from the most commonly listed address (2784B Hwy 11 N). This could indicate a business that has relocated but never updated its listings -- or a business that operates from a home address and uses a different mailing address.

### 3. Zero Google Reviews After 12 Years
Zero reviews after 12 years in business is unusual. Either Ben has never asked for reviews (most likely), his GBP is not properly verified/claimed, or the listing has been reset at some point. This is a red flag for outreach because it could signal that Ben is not interested in growing the business digitally -- he may actively resist marketing.

### 4. Hotmail Email
Using BensHeating@hotmail.com as the business email suggests very low digital sophistication. This business may not be receptive to digital marketing services. The sales conversation would need to be very educational and start from fundamentals.

### 5. GBP Hours Discrepancy
The GBP shows "Closes 10:30 PM" while Yellow Pages and NorthBayDirect show Mon-Fri 8-5. This means either Ben is not managing his GBP at all (most likely), or the listing was auto-generated/claimed by someone else.

### 6. Yellow Pages Review Suppression
The Yellow Pages listing notes that the business "has requested that no ratings & reviews or any third party content be displayed." This is an unusual choice and could suggest Ben is either privacy-conscious or had negative reviews he wanted hidden. Either way, it signals possible resistance to transparency and online reviews.
