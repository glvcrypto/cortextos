## Three Opportunities

### 1. Google Business Profile + Review Engine (Quick Win)
**Keyword:** "sudbury home builders" (90/mo, KD 21) + Maps pack visibility
**Current State:** No GBP exists. Zero reviews anywhere.
**Opportunity:** Creating and optimising a GBP profile would instantly make Northern Elite visible in the Maps 3-pack for local builder searches. Requesting reviews from past clients (2022-2024 builds) could generate 5-10 reviews within the first month.
**Impact:** Maps pack visibility could generate 3-5 qualified leads per month at zero ad cost. With a $500K+ average home build, even one new client per quarter represents significant ROI.
**Evidence:** 91% of homebuyers check reviews. BelMar's Maps presence contributes to their dominant position.

### 2. SEO-Optimised Website Rebuild (Foundation Fix)
**Keyword:** "home builders sudbury ontario" (90/mo, KD 21) -- Northern Elite does not rank for this at all
**Current State:** GoDaddy builder, thin content, no blog, no schema, 8 total backlinks
**Opportunity:** A proper WordPress site with location pages, project case studies, model home pages with real content, blog targeting long-tail keywords ("cost to build a custom home in Sudbury," "raised ranch vs two storey Northern Ontario"), and proper schema markup could capture significant organic traffic.
**Target:** Move from 8 keywords to 50+ within 6 months. BelMar has 109 -- there is plenty of room.
**Evidence:** Northern Elite already ranks #7 for "sudbury home builders" with essentially no effort. A proper site could push into top 3.

### 3. Content Marketing: Raised Ranch Authority (Differentiation Play)
**Keyword:** "raised ranch house" (210/mo, KD low) + "raised ranch home" (140/mo)
**Current State:** Position 38 and 42 for these terms -- from a single page
**Opportunity:** Northern Elite already builds raised ranch homes and has a page that inadvertently ranks. Creating authoritative content around raised ranch homes (floor plans, benefits for Northern Ontario climate, cost comparisons, gallery of completed builds) could position Marcel as THE raised ranch expert in Northern Ontario. This is a niche no local competitor is targeting.
**Impact:** 350/mo combined search volume with low competition. Most searchers are in the consideration stage -- high-intent traffic.
**Evidence:** Only 1 page currently exists and it already ranks in the 30s-40s. Dedicated content cluster could push into top 10.
