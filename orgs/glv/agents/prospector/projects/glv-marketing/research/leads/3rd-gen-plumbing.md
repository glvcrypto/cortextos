## Opportunities Identified

1. **Emergency GBP fix** (data-backed) — The "Website" link on his Google Business Profile currently sends customers to Providence Plumbing (a competitor). This can be fixed in 5 minutes by updating or removing the website URL. This alone is worth the conversation — Eric is literally funding a competitor's lead pipeline through his own GBP.

2. **New website + domain recovery** (data-backed) — 3rdgenplumbingandheating.ca needs to be re-registered (or a new domain secured) and a proper website built. With 27 Google reviews and 4.7 stars already earned, a basic professional site with service pages, emergency contact info, and schema markup would immediately start converting the traffic his GBP already generates. Estimated ~1,070 monthly plumbing searches in North Bay, and he currently captures zero of them online.

3. **Review velocity acceleration** (data-backed) — Eric has 27 Google reviews at 4.7 stars. Perrotta's leads with ~84 at 4.8. Harwood has ~61 at 4.2. Eric is already #3 by review count in the market — a structured review request system (QR code on invoices, follow-up text) could push him to #2 within 6-12 months. His quality score (4.7) is already competitive with the leaders.

4. **Directory blitz** (data-backed) — Missing from Yellow Pages, BBB, HomeStars, TrustedPros, and dozens of other directories where competitors are listed. Basic NAP citations across 15-20 directories would improve local SEO signals even before the website is rebuilt.

5. **Sturgeon Falls content play** (observational) — Eric serves both North Bay and Sturgeon Falls (population ~6,000). Nobody in this market has strong "plumber Sturgeon Falls" content. Service area pages for Sturgeon Falls, Callander, Corbeil, and surrounding communities would capture low-competition long-tail searches.

6. **Camera inspection specialization** (observational) — Eric offers sewer camera inspections (mentioned in Yelp review and DIYoffer listing). This is specialized equipment most small plumbers lack. "Sewer camera inspection North Bay" is an unclaimed keyword with purchase intent. A dedicated page could establish him as the go-to diagnostic plumber.
