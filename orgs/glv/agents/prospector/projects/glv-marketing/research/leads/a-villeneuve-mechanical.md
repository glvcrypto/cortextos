## Personal Story

Tony Villeneuve founded A. Villeneuve Mechanical in 1985, building it from the ground up in Thunder Bay. When Tony passed away, his sons Jeff and Jason took over in 2006, continuing the family legacy into a second generation. They have grown the business from a local plumbing operation to a full mechanical services company handling commercial, industrial, and residential projects across Northwestern Ontario.

Jeff attended Laurentian University in Sudbury and has built a strong professional network (500+ LinkedIn connections). The brothers run a unionized shop (UA Local 628), which speaks to their commitment to fair labour practices and skilled tradework. Their team includes specialized backflow prevention technicians, gas fitters, and plumbers — not just general labourers.
