## Outreach Strategy

### Approach: Direct, Respectful, Results-Focused

Dan Mauro runs a home-based sole proprietorship. He has survived 19 years on reputation alone with zero digital marketing. He is likely:
- Busy with work (doesn't need marketing to stay busy)
- Skeptical of digital marketing ("I get all my work from referrals")
- Price-sensitive (sole operator, not a large firm)
- Potentially interested in growth but unsure where to start

### HOOK Questions (per Ben's framework)

1. "Dan, when someone in Thunder Bay Googles 'plumber near me,' do you know which competitors show up first -- and why you don't?"
2. "You've got 19 years and zero BBB complaints. But right now, the only way a new customer finds you is if someone they know gives them your number. What happens when those referrals slow down?"
3. "Clow Darling gets over 1,400 website visits a month from Google. You get zero. What would even 10% of that traffic mean for your business?"

### Recommended Service Package

**Starter package (website + GBP optimization):**
- 5-page WordPress website (Home, Services, About, Service Area, Contact)
- Google Business Profile setup/optimization with photos
- Review generation strategy (email/text follow-up templates)
- Basic local SEO (NAP cleanup, schema markup)
- Monthly GBP posts

**Why this works for Dan:** Low commitment, high impact. He doesn't need a $5K build. He needs to exist online. A clean, professional site with an optimized GBP would immediately put him on the map for "plumber Thunder Bay" searches.
