## Personal Story

Dan Stars founded Stellar after witnessing larger HVAC companies overcharge and underdeliver. He saw customers being treated as numbers rather than people, and as a skilled tradesman himself, he knew it could be done better. He built Stellar around three principles:

1. **Honest pricing** — no hidden fees, transparent quoting
2. **Expert workmanship** — "we treat every home like it's our own"
3. **Genuine care** — direct access to the owner, 24/7 availability

In his own words: *"Great service isn't just about getting the job done — it's about doing it right, with respect, integrity and transparency."*

The "underdog vs. big corporations" narrative is central to his brand identity. His tagline — "Built for Northern Living. Trusted by Your Neighbours" — reinforces the local, community-first positioning.

**Key motivator:** Dan appears driven by doing right by people and proving that a small local operator can outperform the big guys. This is someone who takes personal pride in every install.

## Opportunities Identified

### 1. Local SEO — Fix Schema & Dominate Map Pack
The empty address fields in their schema markup are leaving rankings on the table. Proper LocalBusiness schema with complete NAP data, service area markup, and FAQ schema would be quick wins.

### 2. Content Strategy — Turn Blog Into Lead Engine
27 thin posts are doing almost nothing for SEO. A proper keyword-targeted content strategy with 1,000-1,500 word posts on high-intent queries ("furnace replacement cost Sault Ste Marie," "heat pump rebates Ontario 2026," "best HVAC company Algoma") would drive organic leads.

### 3. Owner Story / About Page
Dan's story is compelling — tradesman frustrated with corporate HVAC overcharging, builds his own company, grows to 10 employees in 2 years. This story is NOWHERE on the website. An About page with Dan's face, story, and values would massively boost trust and conversion.

### 4. Social Media — Video Content
HVAC is perfect for short-form video — install walkthroughs, "day in the life," winter emergency tips, before/after transformations. Dan's personality (direct, honest, hands-on) would play well on video.

### 5. Review Generation System
52 reviews in ~2 years is solid but could be systematized. At 10 employees doing multiple jobs per week, they should be generating 10-15 reviews/month with a proper follow-up system.

### 6. STELLARCare Marketing
Their membership program is a recurring revenue goldmine but appears undermarketed. Dedicated landing page, email nurture sequences, and retargeting ads could significantly grow membership sign-ups.

### 7. Duplicate URL Cleanup
Multiple URLs serving similar content will cause cannibalization issues as they scale.
