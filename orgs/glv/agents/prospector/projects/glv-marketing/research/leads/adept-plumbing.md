## Red Flags

1. **Duplicate GBP listing is actively harming rankings** -- This is the single biggest issue. Google may be suppressing both listings due to confusion. The 4.1-star duplicate drags down perceived quality.
2. **2-page website signals "not serious"** -- To a homeowner comparing Adept (2 pages) vs. Clow Darling (full site with team, services, emergency info), Clow Darling wins every time on perceived professionalism.
3. **No emergency messaging in a -30C market** -- Thunder Bay homeowners' #1 plumbing fear is frozen/burst pipes. Adept doesn't even mention emergency service.
4. **Gmail on Facebook vs. professional email on website** -- Suggests ad-hoc digital presence management, not a deliberate strategy.
5. **Small team risk** -- With only Trevor and Andrew visible, capacity may be a concern. Marketing growth needs to be paced with hiring.
6. **No hours on website** -- Customers who find the site outside business hours have no idea if they can get emergency help.
7. **Squarespace limitations** -- Limited SEO control, no custom schema, slower than WordPress with proper caching. Migration will be needed for serious SEO work.
