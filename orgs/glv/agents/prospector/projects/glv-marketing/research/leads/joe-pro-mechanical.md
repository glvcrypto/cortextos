This is where the outreach hook lives.

- Emergency plumbing decisions happen fast — people search, check reviews, and call within minutes
- A 1.8-star rating with a complaint about shoddy work and property damage is an **active repellent**

**What this means for Joe:**
- Every potential customer who Googles "Joe Pro Mechanical Thunder Bay" finds one review describing dropped water tanks, dented doors, and shoddy work

**The invisible cost:** Joe doesn't know how many jobs he's losing. A homeowner who Googles, sees the 1.8 rating, and calls Clow Darling instead never contacts Joe to tell him he lost the job. This is silent revenue bleed.
