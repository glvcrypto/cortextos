### Marketing Assessment

#### Current State: 2/10

**What they have:**
- A domain (allaspectslimited.com)
- A phone number that is consistent across listings
- A Google Business Profile (exists)
- A Facebook page (exists)
- An Instagram account (exists)
- Positive word-of-mouth (reviews mention Andrew by name)

**What they are missing (critical gaps):**

1. **No real website** — Single-page GoDaddy builder with ~150 words. No service pages, no about page, no blog, no contact form. This is essentially a digital business card.

2. **Zero directory listings** — Not on YellowPages, HomeStars, BBB, Chamber of Commerce, or ANY major Canadian business directory. This means no citation signals for local SEO.

3. **No address published** — "Thunder Bay, ON" is not a NAP-compliant address. Google needs a street address (or service-area declaration) for local pack ranking.

4. **Negligible review volume** — ~3-5 Google reviews vs. competitors with dozens. No review generation strategy.

5. **No content strategy** — Zero blog posts, zero landing pages, zero keyword targeting. They rank for 6 keywords by accident (domain name + GBP).

6. **No schema markup** — No LocalBusiness, no Service, no Review schema.

7. **No backflow/camera inspection content** — These are high-intent, low-competition service searches they should own.

8. **GoDaddy Website Builder** — Poor SEO capabilities, limited customization, slow performance, no blog.

#### Opportunities (ranked by impact)

1. **Build a real website** — 8-10 service pages + about + contact + blog. WordPress or modern stack. Immediate organic visibility boost.

2. **Directory blitz** — Submit to 15+ Canadian directories (YellowPages, HomeStars, BBB, Chamber, 411.ca, etc.). Each consistent citation = stronger local SEO signal.

3. **Google Business Profile optimization** — Complete address, service categories, business hours, photos, posts, Q&A. Target Local Pack.

4. **Review generation campaign** — Systematic ask after every job. Goal: 20+ Google reviews in 90 days.

5. **Service-specific landing pages** — "sewer camera inspection thunder bay", "backflow testing thunder bay", "drain cleaning thunder bay" — all low-competition, high-intent.

6. **Content marketing** — Blog posts on common plumbing issues, seasonal maintenance, backflow regulations. Build topical authority.

7. **Schema markup** — LocalBusiness + Service + FAQ schema on every page.
