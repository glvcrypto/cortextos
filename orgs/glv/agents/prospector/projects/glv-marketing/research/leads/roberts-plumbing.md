## Personal Story

The Squitti family is one of Thunder Bay's established Italian-Canadian families, rooted in Secord Street since 1915 when Joseph Squitti, a woodworker from Italy, opened a grocery store behind the Italian Hall. The family built multiple businesses from that same block — Squitti's "A Beautiful Difference" department store (1975-present) at Bay & Secord, and Robert's Plumbing at 286 Secord.

Robert's Plumbing was founded in 1957 and has operated continuously for 69 years, making it one of the longest-running trade businesses in Thunder Bay. The current generation appears to include a younger Robert Squitti who earned a bilingual diploma and studied HVAC at George Brown College in Toronto before returning to the family business, and Norm Squitti who handles dealer relationships and operations.

The family's community roots run deep: Tammy Squitti (Robert's wife) chairs the Nanabijou Childcare Centre board and volunteers with Special Olympics Ontario through the Thunder Bay Police Service. Caesar Squitti (related family member, deceased 2021) was a 25+ year member of the Italian Society of Port Arthur and built "Thunder Bay On-line," a community cultural magazine.

This is a family that has literally built their life on one street in Thunder Bay for over a century. Their plumbing business is part of that legacy.

## Opportunities Identified

1. **Capture ~1,354/mo in organic traffic (data-backed).** Clow Darling ranks for 175 keywords generating ~1,354 monthly visits. Robert's ranks for 1 keyword with 0 traffic. A proper website with service pages, location pages, and blog content could capture 25-40% of the ~4,375/mo total addressable search volume within 12 months. At a 3-5% conversion rate, that's 30-65 new leads/month from organic alone.

2. **Push Google rating above 4.3 stars with review management.** Robert's has 79-134 reviews but a 3.7-3.9 average with zero owner responses. Implementing a review response strategy + proactive review requests from satisfied customers could lift the rating above 4.3 within 3-6 months. Every 0.1-star increase above 4.0 correlates with ~5% more clicks on GBP.

3. **Online booking / web forms to stop the phone leak.** The most common complaint is unreturned calls. Adding online booking, web contact forms, and automated confirmation emails solves the #1 customer complaint while generating trackable leads. This is the easiest win and addresses their biggest vulnerability.

4. **"69 Years on Secord Street" brand story content.** No competitor in Thunder Bay plumbing has a 69-year, multi-generational family story rooted in the Italian-Canadian community. This is a powerful differentiator for the 35-65+ demographic that values trust and reliability. Blog content, About page storytelling, and video would convert this legacy into digital authority.

5. **Emergency service landing pages (competitor comparison).** Thunder Bay's harsh winters drive high-urgency searches ("emergency plumber Thunder Bay", "frozen pipes Thunder Bay", "furnace repair Thunder Bay"). Robert's advertises 24-hour emergency service but has zero web presence for these terms. Clow Darling likely captures this traffic. Purpose-built landing pages with click-to-call would directly monetize Robert's existing 24/7 capability.

6. **Local SEO / directory cleanup.** Inconsistent phone numbers across directories ((807) 345-9353 vs (807) 623-4235), unclaimed profiles on multiple platforms, and a hotmail email on the Trane dealer profile all signal neglected local SEO. A NAP consistency sweep + claiming all profiles would improve GBP ranking signals.

## Recommended Outreach Angle

**Lead with empathy about the phone problem, not a sales pitch about SEO.**

The strongest hook is: "I noticed something that might be costing you customers — and it's fixable." The pattern of unanswered calls showing up across BBB, Google, and Yelp reviews is a tangible, observable problem that any business owner would care about. It's not abstract ("your SEO is bad") — it's concrete ("people are writing reviews saying they called 6 times").

**Angle:** "Your 69-year reputation is earning you calls — but the reviews suggest some of those calls aren't getting answered. We help businesses like yours make sure every call turns into a customer, whether that's through online booking, web forms, or making sure Google sends the right people to you in the first place. Right now, Clow Darling is capturing about 1,350 searches a month that you're not showing up for."

**Personalization hooks:**
- The Squitti family legacy on Secord Street (shows we did our homework)
- The Trane dealership (shows we understand their business model)
- The ThreeBestRated #1 ranking (positive framing — "you're already the best, you're just invisible online")
- Bilingual capability (younger Robert has a bilingual diploma — potential French-language content angle for Franco-Ontarian market)

**Contact strategy:** Email first to normsquitti@hotmail.com and robertsplumbing@tbaytel.net (phone may go unanswered based on pattern). Follow up in person at 286 Secord St if no response.
