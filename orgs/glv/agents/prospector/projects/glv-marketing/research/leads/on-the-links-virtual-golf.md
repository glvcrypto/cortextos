## Personal Story

Chris Ferguson Jr. is a Thunder Bay native who left home as a teenager to play hockey in Michigan and Ontario's OHL. After hockey, he completed a Business Management degree from Western University in London, Ontario, building experience in sales, food and beverage, human resources, and general business management.

His entrepreneurial drive was inspired by watching his family operate a successful business for over 40 years in Thunder Bay. After extensive research — including traveling to several golf facility manufacturers — Chris and his family decided to transform the former Quality Market grocery store on Golf Links Road into Thunder Bay's first virtual golf and sports bar.

On The Links opened in fall 2019, and the community response was immediate: Ferguson reported 57 of a possible 60 simulator hours booked on one Friday shortly after opening. The location is strategically positioned — "central, right off the highway and easily accessible," neighbouring Thunder Bay Country Club (1130 Golf Links Rd), Lakehead University, Confederation College, and the Thunder Bay Regional Health Sciences Centre.

The business survived COVID (opening just months before the pandemic) and has since expanded to include Lento Wood Fired Pizza as an on-site food partner, golf camps for juniors/teens, league play, and corporate events. Chris personally handles bookings, communications, and much of the social media, using his personal cell number (807-632-3892) publicly for text/call reservations.
