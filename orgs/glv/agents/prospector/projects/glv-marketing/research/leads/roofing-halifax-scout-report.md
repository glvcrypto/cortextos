## Niche Research — Halifax Roofing

**Maritime climate demand drivers:** Halifax experiences Atlantic ice storms (ice damming endemic), hurricane-track windstorms (Fiona 2022 is the local benchmark), and persistent salt-air corrosion. This drives above-average replacement cycles — shingles degrade faster than inland Ontario. Strong demand for metal/steel as premium alternative. Peak inquiry windows: spring (April–June, post-winter damage) and fall (September–October, pre-freeze). Ice and water shield up-sell is standard on every job.

**Top 3 organic results ("roofing Halifax NS", "roof replacement Halifax"):**

1. **Leverman Roofing** (levermanroofing.com) — Top 1–2 across all aggregators. GAF Master Elite certified, founded 1987 (38+ yrs), BBB A+. Dominant authority signal. **Active Tier A — pre-check flag.**
2. **Above the Rest Permanent Roofing Systems** (abovetherestroofing.ca) — ThreeBestRated #2. 40+ yrs combined experience, family-owned, Canadian-made products.
3. **Refined Roofing** (refinedroofing.com) — 91 Google reviews @ 4.8★, top-10 on every aggregator. 12+ years, family partnership.

**Franchise / national chain presence:** Almost entirely commercial/institutional — no residential national franchise blockers. CentiMark (commercial only), Atlas-Apex (15 offices Canada-wide, commercial only), Flynn Group (35 offices, commercial/cladding), Rydel Roofing (franchise network — 12+ locations). Independent operators dominate the residential/light-commercial space.
