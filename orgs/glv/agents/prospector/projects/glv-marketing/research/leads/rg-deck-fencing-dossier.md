But his online presence tells a completely different story. **The gap between his real-world business quality and his digital footprint is enormous:**

Sudbury is the gateway to cottage country (French River, Lake Nipissing, Manitoulin Island). RG already offers dock installation/removal — a perfect cottage country service — but has zero content targeting this audience.

Keywords like "dock installation near me" (40/mo, $3.07 CPC) and "deck builder near me" (590/mo, $3.70 CPC) represent high-intent searches from both residential and cottage owners. RG is invisible for all of them.

This is a market where a modest investment in SEO and content could achieve dominant positioning within 6-12 months — before competitors wake up.
