## Opportunities

### 1. NAP Consistency Cleanup (Quick Win)
Two different addresses across directories. Duplicate Facebook pages. Duplicate HomeStars listings. This is low-hanging fruit that directly impacts local SEO rankings.

### 2. Website Overhaul (Critical)
The site has no phone number or address on the contact page. This is a conversion disaster. The site was last updated in 2021. It's built on Wix with minimal SEO capability. A modern WordPress site with proper schema markup, service pages, and a blog would transform their digital presence.

### 3. Content Marketing / Blog (Massive Gap)
Zero blog content on a site for a business with 27 years of expertise. Chris has the knowledge and the storytelling ability (proven by his Mechanical Hub feature). Topics like "radiant floor heating in Northern Ontario," "hydronic heating vs forced air," "choosing a plumber for your custom home build" would dominate low-competition Thunder Bay keywords.

### 4. Google Business Profile Optimization
Only ~6 reviews after 27 years. They need a systematic review generation strategy. GBP posts, Q&A, service categories, photos — all likely under-optimized.

### 5. "Plumbers Thunder Bay" Keyword (590 vol, position 33)
This is the money keyword. They're on page 4. With proper on-page SEO, a dedicated service area page, and consistent NAP, they could reach page 1. Clow Darling likely ranks higher here.

### 6. Personal Brand Leverage
Chris already has 1,146 posts on @nwo_plumber and a feature in 3 trade publications. His personal brand is strong but completely disconnected from the business website. Connecting the two (team page, author bio on blog, embedded Instagram feed) would add massive E-E-A-T signals.

### 7. Hydronic Heating Niche Domination
Thunder Bay's harsh winters (avg. -14.8C in January) + 73% pre-1980 housing stock = massive demand for heating upgrades. Aqua North's Uponor certification and hydronic heating specialization is a unique differentiator that no competitor is marketing effectively.

### 8. Co-op / Community Angle
Chris mentors co-op students annually. This is an unused content and PR goldmine — community involvement stories, "meet our apprentice" posts, partnerships with local schools. Builds trust in a market where 35-65 year old homeowners value community ties.
