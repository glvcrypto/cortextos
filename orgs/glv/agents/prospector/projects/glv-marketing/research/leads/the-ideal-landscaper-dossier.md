## Opportunities Identified

### 1. Website Overhaul (High Impact)
Wix site with minimal content, no blog, limited SEO optimization. A proper website with service pages, a materials catalogue, and local SEO content would capture significantly more of the 1,300+ monthly searches for "landscaper near me" and "landscaping companies near me" where they currently sit at positions 5-13.

### 2. Content Marketing / Blog (High Impact)
Zero blog content. Huge opportunity for seasonal content: spring landscaping tips, gravel/topsoil buying guides, snow removal prep, hardscaping ideas for Northern Ontario. This would build topical authority and capture informational search traffic.

### 3. Google Business Profile Optimization
4.6 stars on only 21 reviews after 30+ years suggests they are not actively asking for reviews. A review generation campaign could quickly push them to 50+ reviews and strengthen their local pack positioning.

### 4. Paid Search (Quick Win)
Zero Google Ads presence. Competitors are likely bidding on "landscaping sault ste marie" and "snow removal sault ste marie." Even a small budget ($500-800/mo) during peak seasons could capture high-intent leads immediately.

### 5. Social Media Consolidation
Two separate Facebook pages = diluted presence. Need to merge or redirect, then build a consistent posting schedule with project photos, seasonal tips, and material promotions.

### 6. Email Professionalization
Still using a Yahoo email address. A branded email (e.g., tony@theideallandscaper.com or info@theideallandscaper.com) via Google Workspace would immediately boost credibility.

### 7. Material Sales E-Commerce / Online Ordering
They already offer same-day delivery of materials. An online ordering system for topsoil, gravel, mulch, and stone would be a significant differentiator in the SSM market and capture "buy gravel near me" type searches they already rank for.

## Recommended Outreach Angle

### Primary Why
Tony has built a solid landscaping business over 30 years through word of mouth and hard work. He is ranking well locally (#1 for "landscaping sault ste marie") almost by accident — his Google Business Profile is doing the heavy lifting while his website and overall digital presence are significantly underperforming relative to his actual reputation. **The gap between his real-world reputation and his online presence is the pitch.** ThreeBestRated lists him in the top 3, customers give him 4.6+ stars, but his website and marketing don't reflect a top-tier operation.

### How GLV Helps
1. **Website that matches his reputation** — Replace the basic Wix site with a professional, SEO-optimized site that showcases 30 years of work, his materials catalogue, and generates leads year-round.
