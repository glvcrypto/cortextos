## Red Flags

1. **WEBSITE COMPLETELY BROKEN** — Infinite redirect loop. Domain appears to redirect to competitor sites (Be The Ball / Lively Golf Club). This is either a domain expiry, DNS misconfiguration, or hosting failure. They may not even know.
2. **No online booking** — Every competitor has it. Phone-only booking in 2026 is a major friction point, especially for younger golfers.
3. **NAP inconsistency** — 4+ name variations across directories. Hurts local SEO.
4. **Very low review volume** — Single-digit Google reviews vs. competitors who likely have dozens.
5. **Legacy email addresses** — tutanota.com and sympatico.ca suggest minimal tech adoption.
6. **Unclear Sunday hours** — Conflicting info across directories.
7. **Chelmsford location** — 20 min outside Sudbury core. All competitors are in Sudbury proper. Geographic disadvantage for walk-in/impulse visits.
8. **Possible domain loss** — The redirect to Be The Ball (a direct competitor) is deeply concerning. If the domain was lost, someone else now controls it.

## Three Opportunities

### 1. Emergency Website + Domain Recovery (URGENT)
**The problem:** Their website has been broken (possibly for months/years) with an infinite redirect loop that sends visitors to competitor websites. They are literally funnelling potential customers to Be The Ball and Lively Golf Club.
**The fix:** New website on a fresh domain or recovered domain. Mobile-first design with online booking integration, service pages targeting "golf simulator sudbury," "custom club fitting sudbury," "golf lessons chelmsford."
**The hook:** "Right now, anyone who types your web address into their browser gets sent to your competitor's website. Every single one."
**Revenue impact:** At 390 searches/month for "golf simulator sudbury" alone, even capturing 15% would be 58 new visitors/month. At $27 avg/hr simulator rate, even 10 bookings/month = $3,240/year in recovered revenue minimum.

### 2. Google Business Profile Optimization + Review Generation
**The problem:** 5.0 rating but single-digit reviews. Competitors with more reviews and complete profiles will outrank them in map pack results for "indoor golf near me," "golf simulator sudbury," etc.
**The fix:** Complete GBP optimization (photos, services, Q&A, posts), implement a review generation system (QR code at checkout, follow-up text after sessions), and build up to 50+ reviews within 6 months.
**The hook:** "You've had loyal customers for 25 years. Most of them have never left you a Google review. That's 25 years of social proof that's invisible to new customers."

### 3. Local SEO Content Strategy: "Sudbury's Original Indoor Golf"
**The problem:** With no website, they have zero organic search visibility. Competitors are capturing all search traffic.
**The fix:** Content targeting the full keyword cluster: "golf simulator sudbury" (390/mo), "indoor golf sudbury," "golf lessons sudbury," "custom club fitting northern ontario," "driving range chelmsford." Leverage Rene's World Top 100 ClubFitter credentials as an authority differentiator no competitor can match.
