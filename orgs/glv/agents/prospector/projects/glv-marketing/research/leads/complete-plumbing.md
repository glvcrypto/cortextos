## Opportunities Identified

1. **Google Business Profile setup** (data-backed) — Zero GBP in a market where the leader has only 84 reviews. Even 10-15 reviews would make him competitive for Maps Pack placement across ~1,070 monthly plumbing searches.

2. **SSL + website overhaul** (data-backed) — Free SSL via Let's Encrypt would fix the "Not Secure" warning. Rebuild with service pages, service area pages for each of 10 communities, schema markup, and real testimonials.

3. **Review generation** (observational) — 1 review in 5+ years despite apparently good work. Simple QR code on invoices or follow-up text could build review base quickly. Perrotta's has 84 — even 20 would be top 5 in this market.

4. **NAP cleanup + directory expansion** (data-backed) — Resolve address inconsistency, build citations on 15+ directories where he's absent (HomeStars, BBB, Houzz, TrustedPros, etc.)

5. **Service area content** (competitor comparison) — 10-community service area with zero location-specific content. Nobody in this market is doing "Plumber in Powassan" or "Emergency Plumbing Sturgeon Falls" pages.
