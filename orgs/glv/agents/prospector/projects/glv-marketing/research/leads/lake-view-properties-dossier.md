## Personal Story

Lake View Properties is a two-founder operation built on complementary strengths. Shane Arnold brings 24 years of construction experience — he is the veteran builder, the one who has been swinging hammers and managing job sites since the early 2000s. Steven O'Brien comes from a different path: a decade in construction and carpentry, but also 10 years running an award-winning gym in Sudbury and 10+ years as a volunteer firefighter. Steven's background signals discipline, community involvement, and entrepreneurial grit. Together they represent the classic Northern Ontario partnership: one partner with deep trade expertise, the other with business acumen and community roots.

Their tagline "Quality. Every time." and their structured four-stage build process (Design Questionnaire, Construction Agreement, Build Concept, Finalize Plan) suggest they take a methodical, client-focused approach. They hired The Agency Creative Co. (Sudbury Advertising) to build their website, which shows they are willing to invest in their brand. The creative domain "buildyour.homes" is memorable but unusual — it signals ambition to stand out, but may actually be hurting them in search (more on that below).

The fact that they cover Greater Sudbury, Manitoulin Island, AND Muskoka District is notable — that is a massive geographic footprint for a small builder. Either they have serious capacity, or they are casting a wide net hoping to fill their pipeline. Given the zero Google reviews and near-zero online visibility, the latter seems more likely.

## Big-Picture Opportunity

The Sudbury custom home market (~2,920 searches/month across related terms) is underserved digitally. Even BelMar, the local leader, only has 109 keywords and 204 monthly visits — this is NOT a saturated market. The top keywords are achievable:

- "home builders sudbury ontario" (90/mo, BelMar #2, Lake View #21)
- "sudbury home builders" (90/mo, BelMar #3, Lake View #21)
- "custom homes sudbury" (20/mo — nobody owns this yet)

Lake View Properties has a **unique positioning advantage**: they serve Sudbury + Manitoulin Island + Muskoka. No other Sudbury builder markets this geographic range. Content targeting "custom home builder Manitoulin Island" or "cottage builder Muskoka" would face near-zero competition.

Steven's firefighter background and Shane's 24 years of experience are powerful trust signals that are currently invisible online. A content strategy highlighting these personal stories would differentiate Lake View from cookie-cutter builder websites.

## Three Opportunities

### 1. Google Business Profile + Review Engine (Quick Win — 30 days)
Complete the GBP profile (photos, services, posts, address). Implement a systematic review request process targeting past clients. Getting to even 10 reviews at 5.0 stars would dramatically improve Map Pack visibility. **Cost to them: $0 if they do it themselves, or included in an SEO retainer.** This alone could generate their first inbound leads from Google.

### 2. Local SEO Foundation (60-90 days)
Submit to 20+ directories (HomeStars, Houzz, SDHBA, Yellow Pages, BBB, Yelp, TrustedPros, etc.). Build legitimate backlinks to replace the 100% spam profile. Create neighbourhood/service-area landing pages (Sudbury, Manitoulin, Muskoka). Target "custom home builder [location]" for each service area. This would move them from #21 to page 1 for core terms.

### 3. Content Strategy — Project Showcases + Builder Education (90-180 days)
Monthly blog posts featuring completed projects with photo galleries, process breakdowns, and client stories. Educational content about building in Northern Ontario (permits, lot selection, winterization, rebates). This captures research-phase buyers and builds the topical authority that Google rewards. BelMar's neighbourhood pages prove this works locally.
