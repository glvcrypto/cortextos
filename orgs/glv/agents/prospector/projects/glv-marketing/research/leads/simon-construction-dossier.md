## Opportunities

### 1. Website Resurrection (Urgent / Foundation)
Both domains are dead. The immediate priority is getting a functional, SEO-optimized website live. This alone would differentiate Simon Construction from its current state of digital invisibility. A 5-7 page site (Home, Services, About, Gallery, Contact) with proper local SEO targeting "general contractor sudbury" (210/mo, KD 15) could start ranking within 2-3 months given the low competition. **This is the conversation starter -- Marc almost certainly does not know his site is down.**

### 2. Google Business Profile + Review Generation
With 91% of consumers checking reviews, Simon's 2 reviews (one negative) are devastating. A claimed and optimized GBP with a systematic review generation strategy targeting past satisfied customers could transform his online reputation within 60 days. Even 10-15 genuine 5-star reviews would make him competitive. The existing positive review proves customers are willing to vouch for Marc -- he just needs to ask.

### 3. HomeStars / Directory Domination
Simon Construction is absent from HomeStars, Houzz, and BBB -- the platforms Sudbury homeowners actively use to find contractors. Getting listed (free) on these platforms with consistent NAP, photos, and reviews would create multiple touchpoints for potential customers. Combined with cleaning up the inconsistent NAP data across existing listings (two domains, two phone numbers), this would build a professional digital footprint without a large budget.
