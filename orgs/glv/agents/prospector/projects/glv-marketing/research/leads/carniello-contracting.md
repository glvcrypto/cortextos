## Personal Story

Stefano and Mirko Carniello are brothers who founded Carniello Contracting in 2005. The Carniello name is Italian in origin — consistent with Sudbury's deep Italian-Canadian community (Sudbury has one of the largest Italian populations in Northern Ontario, dating to the mining boom). The company lists Italian alongside English as a service language.

Stefano studied Civil Engineering Technology at Cambrian College (Sudbury's local college) and worked as an estimator at Interpaving — a well-known Sudbury paving and site-servicing firm established in 1972 — before going out on his own. This background suggests he learned the business from the ground up: technical education, then industry experience, then entrepreneurship.

The business is Tarion-registered (mandatory for new home builders in Ontario), a member of the Sudbury & District Home Builders Association, and a member of the Greater Sudbury Chamber of Commerce. These memberships indicate a legitimate, established operation that takes compliance seriously.

Their Instagram (387 posts, 515 followers) shows active project documentation — custom homes in Blezard Valley and surrounding Sudbury communities. Their Houzz profile showcases two named projects: "1002 Valecrest" and "1042 Cloverdale" (both Blezard Valley).

No awards, press coverage, or community event involvement found in public records. No corporate acquisitions or chain affiliations detected — this is an independent family business.
