## Personal Story

Mark Sharkey comes from a deeply rooted North Bay family with a tradition of community service. His father, **Charles Sharkey**, was a City of North Bay firefighter for over **31 years**, passing in April 2021 at age 86 after 65 years of marriage to Jean (nee Cundari). His brother **Brian Sharkey** passed in February 2023 at approximately age 63. Mark has siblings Dean (Kim) and Jo-Ann O'Neil (Tim).

Mark founded Sharkey's Plumbing & Heating on **May 17, 1994** — the same year his father would have been wrapping up his firefighting career. In a very real sense, Mark carried on the family tradition of serving North Bay — just with pipes instead of hoses.

After 31 years, Sharkey's remains a sole proprietorship. No franchise. No private equity. No corporate owner. Just Mark, his phone, and a reputation built one job at a time.

**Copywriter note:** The "firefighter's son who became a plumber" angle is genuine, verifiable, and emotionally resonant. No competitor in North Bay has this story.
