## Red Flags

1. **Not a standalone business** — PinHi is an amenity inside Overtime. Marketing spend decisions likely compete with other Overtime priorities (food promos, event bookings, live entertainment). Budget may be minimal.

2. **Single bay = low revenue ceiling** — At $70/hr, max 13hrs/day = $910/day theoretical max. Realistic utilization maybe 30-40% = $270-$360/day = ~$8K-$11K/month gross. After GOLFZON licensing, electricity, staff, the margin is thin. This limits what they'd spend on marketing.

3. **No digital footprint beyond the website** — No GBP, no social media, no reviews. This means zero local SEO presence for "golf simulator Sudbury" (390 searches/mo). They are invisible to Google searchers.

4. **Saturated market** — 6 competitors in a mid-size city. Offszn also has GOLFZON tech. Shooters undercuts on price significantly.

5. **Decision maker complexity** — Law Enterprises owns 20+ businesses. Marketing decisions may be slow or deprioritized. Attilio may not have budget authority for a monthly retainer.

6. **No club rentals** — Limits casual/tourist market. Walk-in bar patrons likely don't have golf clubs with them.

7. **Phone shared with Overtime** — Calls about golf sim get mixed with bar reservations and general inquiries.

### 1. Google Business Profile Setup + Local SEO (HIGHEST IMPACT)

**The problem:** "golf simulator sudbury" gets 390 searches/month and PinHi is INVISIBLE. No GBP listing, no local pack presence, no organic rankings.

**The fix:**
- Create a dedicated Google Business Profile for "PinHi Indoor Golf" at 1051 Kingsway

- Build citations on golf directories, Sudbury tourism sites, recreation directories
- Schema markup on pinhi.ca (LocalBusiness, SportsActivityLocation)
- Target keywords: "golf simulator sudbury," "indoor golf sudbury," "GOLFZON sudbury"

**Expected impact:** With proper GBP optimization, PinHi could capture a significant share of 390 monthly searches. Currently ZERO of those searchers find PinHi through Google.

### 2. Social Media Launch + Content Strategy

**The problem:** Zero social presence. Competitors (Offszn, Be The Ball, Shooters) all have active Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.

**The fix:**
- Launch @pinhi.ca or @pinhisudbury on Instagram and Facebook
- Content pillars: swing clips, GOLFZON tech demos, bar atmosphere, course previews, "did you know" golf facts
- Cross-promote with Overtime's existing social accounts (which ARE active)
- User-generated content from golfers sharing their sim stats
- Short-form video (Reels/TikTok) of the moving swing plate — it's genuinely impressive tech that sells itself visually

### 3. Differentiation Strategy — "Premium Golf Experience" Positioning

**The problem:** PinHi charges $70/hr (highest in market) with 1 bay (fewest in market). They need to justify the premium convincingly.

**The fix:**
- Lean into "quality over quantity" — private experience, no waiting, bar service during play
- Corporate event packages (team building, client entertainment)
- Date night / couples packages (golf + dinner at Overtime)
- League night (even with 1 bay, a weekly league creates recurring revenue and community)
