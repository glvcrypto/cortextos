## Current Marketing Assessment

### Website (girouxhomes.com)

**CRITICAL FINDINGS:**

1. **Footer displays wrong business branding.** The homepage footer reads: "© 2026 RG Deck & Fencing Inc - Sudbury's #1 Deck & Fence Builders Since 2013." RG Deck & Fencing is a separate company owned by Richard Gervais. This means Giroux's website was likely built by the same developer who copied a template and failed to update the footer. This is a serious credibility issue -- any homeowner who scrolls to the bottom sees another company's name and copyright.

2. **JavaScript redirect issues on mobile.** During Playwright testing, resizing the viewport to mobile dimensions caused the page to redirect to rgdeckandfencing.ca/contact/ and various Google Maps searches for other Sudbury contractors (Lavigne Construction, A3 Construction, Gill HS Renovations). This is catastrophic for mobile users -- the site literally sends them to competitors. This is likely caused by a malicious or broken script injection, or a severely misconfigured shared hosting environment.

3. **Homepage hero mismatch.** The hero image shows a deck/backyard scene with the headline "Attention Sudburians! Want your backyard to look like this?" -- promoting deck building, not kitchen/bathroom renovations, which are the company's stated core services and highest-value keywords.

4. **French-language URL slugs.** Several important pages use French path segments: `/notre-expertise/roofing-sudbury`, `/our-prestations/bathroom-renovation-downtown-sudbury`, `/zone-de-service/deck-builder-downtown-sudbury`. This is likely left over from a bilingual theme template and creates confusing, non-standard URLs for an English-language business.

5. **"Widget Area 2" placeholder visible on homepage.** The footer area displays "WIDGET AREA 2 - Click here to assign a widget to this area." -- an unfinished template placeholder visible to all visitors.

## Niche Misalignment

Giroux Property Maintenance operates in the Sudbury home renovation niche with approximately 2,920 combined monthly searches across core terms. The business has 19 organic keywords, 123 referring domains, and holds a #1 position for "bathroom renovation downtown sudbury." These are genuine signals of a real business with real demand.

**The gap between their actual capability and their online presence is severe:**

1. **The website actively promotes competitors.** On mobile (60%+ of traffic), the site redirects to rgdeckandfencing.ca and Google Maps searches for other Sudbury contractors. Every mobile visitor is literally being sent to competitors. This alone could be costing dozens of leads per month.

2. **Wrong company branding in the footer.** "© 2026 RG Deck & Fencing Inc" destroys trust for anyone who notices. It signals "this contractor can't even get his own website right" -- the last thing a homeowner wants to see before handing over $30,000+ for a kitchen renovation.

3. **#1 for the wrong keyword variant.** They rank #1 for "bathroom renovation downtown sudbury" (90/mo, zero competition) but nowhere for "bathroom renovation sudbury" (320/mo, KD 14). The "downtown" modifier limits their reach to a fraction of the total search volume. With proper on-page optimization, they could capture the 320/mo main term.

4. **Keyword cannibalization across service pages.** Three pages are competing against each other in Google. The bathroom page ranks for deck terms, the deck page ranks for bathroom terms, and the siding page ranks for bathroom terms. This dilutes the authority of every page.

5. **Zero review collection strategy.** With 91% of homeowners checking reviews, operating with essentially zero reviews across major platforms means they are invisible to the vast majority of comparison shoppers in Sudbury.

6. **Two phone numbers confuse local signals.** Google cannot determine the canonical phone number when directories show 705-805-4410 and the website shows 705-691-7863. This weakens GBP performance and can cause call tracking issues.
