## Personal Story

Multi-generational family operation. The Beaulieu family acquired Marshall Brothers Industries (est. 1945, sheet metal fabrication) and operates both businesses from the same 9,000 sq ft facility at 153 Ferris Drive. The plumbing business expanded into heating and AC to offer complete mechanical services. Their fabrication capability (TIG welding, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, galvanized) gives them an edge for industrial/commercial projects — custom stainless steel countertops, restaurant hoods, boat repairs, heavy equipment work. They serve clients across Northern Ontario.

The "NOW HIRING" banner on their website suggests the business is active and growing.

## Opportunities Identified

1. **Google Business Profile creation** (competitor comparison) — The ONLY established plumber in North Bay with 25+ years and NO GBP. Perrotta's has 84 reviews, Harwood has 131. Even 15 reviews would make Beaulieu's competitive in Map Pack for ~1,070 monthly searches.

2. **Website rebuild with SSL and local SEO** (data-backed) — Only 8-10 of 28 plumbers have functional websites. A modern site with SSL, service pages for 29 identified keywords (low difficulty), and schema markup would be transformative. Unique differentiator: industrial fabrication capability no competitor can match.

3. **Review generation campaign** (data-backed) — 25+ years of customers. 47% of consumers skip businesses with <20 reviews. Getting past 20 qualifies them for consideration by nearly half the market they're currently losing.

4. **Dual-brand SEO play** (observational) — Marshall Brothers Industries at same address offers cross-referencing between two GBPs, two websites, complementary services. Legitimate backlinks and doubled local search coverage.
