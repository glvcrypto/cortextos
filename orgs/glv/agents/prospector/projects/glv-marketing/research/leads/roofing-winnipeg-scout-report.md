## Niche Research — Winnipeg Roofing

**Prairie climate demand drivers:** Winnipeg averages 17–20 hail days/year, severe freeze-thaw cycles (-40°C to +35°C), heavy snow loads creating ice dam risk, and spring insurance-claim surges post-hail. This creates high replacement frequency (shingles degrade faster than national average), seasonal demand spikes (April–June post-hail, September–October pre-freeze), strong appetite for HAAG-certified hail specialists, and commercial flat roof maintenance as year-round recurring revenue. Independent operators are typically digitally underinvested despite strong demand.

**Top 3 organic results ("roofing Winnipeg MB", "roof replacement Winnipeg"):**

1. **Integrity Roofing Inc.** — Consumer Choice Award 2026 (back-to-back), ThreeBestRated listed, multi-directory presence. 20+ yrs, Steinbach + Winnipeg + Kenora ON coverage. Independent.
2. **Pristine Roofing & Siding** — Consistently cited across all lists. Founded 1998, 15,000+ homes, COR certified, HAAG certified. Strong authority signals.
3. **All Weather Exteriors** — 49 Google reviews @ 4.8★, top-10 across aggregators, 18+ yrs. Heavy on-page SEO content (neighbourhood targeting).

**Runner-up organic presence:** RonOvations (128 Google reviews @ 4.9★), Bjornson Roofing (170 GuildQuality reviews @ 4.9★, only OC Platinum contractor in MB), Charleswood Roofing (136 Birdeye @ 4.8★), Krueger Enterprises (105 Birdeye @ 4.6★).

**Franchise / chain presence:** CentiMark (Winnipeg office, commercial only — Tier C). No residential national franchise chains identified. Market is dominated by independent operators — favorable for GLV outreach.
