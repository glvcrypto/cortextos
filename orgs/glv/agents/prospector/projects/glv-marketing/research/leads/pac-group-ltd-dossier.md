## Personal Story

Paul Caputo is a hometown entrepreneur who started his business at 18 through the Ontario Summer Company program in 2009, launching "Green Grass Yard Care" as a lawn care operation. He was awarded **2009 Young Entrepreneur of the Year** by the local Economic Development Corporation.

Over the next several years, he expanded into excavation, interlock stone, aggregate supply, and commercial snow removal — rebranding to PAC Construction Group LTD to reflect the growth. By 2016, he was recognized with the **SSM Chamber of Commerce "Strive Young Professional of the Year"** award.

Paul is a niche-hunter by nature. In 2016, he identified artificial turf as an untapped market in the Sault (quote: *"I just figured I wanted to do something different, I need to find that niche, so I put some money into it"*). He became the only local installer, completing commercial projects (Phi Dental Care, Our Lady of Lourdes school) and residential installs.

He has grown PAC Group to ~14 employees and opened a landscape supply retail operation (Shopify e-commerce) selling mulch, gravel, soil, and stone with local delivery. The company has been voted SSM's #1 landscape company for 4 consecutive years.

**Key traits:** Self-starter, growth-oriented, niche-aware, community-rooted, started young with no handouts.

## Recommended Outreach Angle

### Primary Why
Paul built PAC Group from a lawn mowing side hustle at 18 into SSM's #1 landscape company — but his online presence does not reflect that success. His competitors can outrank him with a basic blog because he has almost zero content. The Shopify store is smart, but it is an island with no SEO traffic feeding it. He is leaving money on the table every spring when homeowners search "landscape supply Sault Ste Marie" or "excavation contractor SSM" and find someone else.

### How GLV Helps
- **Quick win:** Fix the dual-website issue (Squarespace vs Shopify), clean up stale content (Christmas countdown), and establish branded email.
- **Content strategy:** Build out service area pages + seasonal blog content targeting high-intent local keywords. Position Paul's founder story as a trust signal.
- **E-commerce SEO:** Optimize Shopify product pages for local search (e.g., "buy mulch Sault Ste Marie", "gravel delivery SSM").
- **Seasonal campaigns:** Automated email/social campaigns for spring bookings, fall cleanup, snow removal contracts.

### Conversation Starter
