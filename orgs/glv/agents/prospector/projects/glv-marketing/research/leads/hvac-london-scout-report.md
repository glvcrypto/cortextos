## Donaldson Pre-Check Flag

Donaldson Home Services is London's largest independent HVAC company with 2,900+ reviews, 30+ staff, dual dealer partnerships (Trane + Lennox), and multi-city coverage. This level of operation — from a picnic table in 2013 to 10,500 families in 12 years — almost certainly required active marketing investment along the way. **Before building a dossier:**
- Run Semrush: if they rank top-3 for "HVAC London" or "furnace repair London ON," they already have SEO covered
- Check their Google Ads spend (SimilarWeb/SpyFu): if they're running paid traffic, they likely have an agency
- If SEO is weak despite their brand and reviews: the pitch is very strong — 2,900 reviews and Google still doesn't show you first
