## Personal Story

Bayview Golf Course is one of the oldest golf facilities in the Thunder Bay region, established in 1949 — 77 years of history. Located on Highway 587 in Shuniah, about 45 kilometres east of Thunder Bay near Pass Lake, the course offers Lake Superior views and a classic Northern Ontario golf experience.

In 2016, the facility added an 18-hole disc golf course designed by Andrew Bopp and Phil Jamieson, diversifying beyond traditional golf. Disc golf pricing is accessible at $5/round or $10/day, with rental discs available for $3.

The facility has hosted competitive disc golf events including the Northern Ontario Championship, suggesting active community engagement in the disc golf scene. The traditional golf course side appears more quietly operated.

Operating hours are limited: open Thursday through Monday 10AM-6PM, closed Tuesday and Wednesday. This reduced schedule suggests either limited demand or limited staffing capacity.
