## Deep Research Findings

### The Big Picture
B&D Northern Contracting is a small but versatile contractor in the Greater Sudbury market with a genuine competitive advantage: they are a Nelson Homes Authorized Dealer for Northern Ontario. This gives them access to prefabricated home packages that are particularly attractive in Northern Ontario where traditional construction costs are high and the building season is short. Combined with their excavation capabilities, they offer true turnkey service from raw land to finished home.

Their Instagram content (1,900+ followers, 62+ posts) demonstrates they understand the value of visual marketing in construction -- showing real projects, crew at work, and finished products. The "storm of the century" post from April 2022 shows brand personality and work ethic.

### The Marketing Misalignment
The scout report noted "active social but no SEO" -- and the data confirms this is even worse than expected:

1. **Their website is currently sending all traffic to a competitor.** This is not just "no SEO" -- it is actively harmful to their business. Every GBP click, every directory referral, every Instagram bio link tap sends potential customers to Paramount Construction.

2. **They have 16 organic keywords and almost page-1 rankings for high-value terms** ("sudbury contractors" #11, "contractors sudbury ontario" #12) despite having zero SEO effort. This means Google already sees them as relevant -- a functional website with basic optimization could push these into top-5 positions immediately.

3. **Their social media following (1,900+ Instagram, 587 Facebook likes) is strong for a local contractor** but drives zero website traffic because the website is broken. Every social post with a website link is a dead end or worse, a competitor referral.

4. **Only 7 total reviews across all platforms** despite 25+ years combined experience. In a niche where 91% of consumers check reviews, this is a critical gap.

### Personal Story / Decision Maker Angle
Jeff Brunton is a trades-educated Northern Ontario professional with formal heavy equipment training from both Cambrian College and Sault College. His background is in the dirt -- literally. He understands excavators, trenching, and site work at a mechanical level. The company name "B&D" suggests a partnership (Brunton + D?), and the PO Box in Azilda (a small community in Greater Sudbury) indicates a working-class, boots-on-the-ground operation rather than a corporate contracting firm.

The Nelson Homes dealership is a smart business move -- it gives B&D a product line that differentiates them from other general contractors who only offer labour. Recent Instagram content (1 month ago) actively promotes Nelson Homes packages, suggesting this is a growth area they are investing in.
