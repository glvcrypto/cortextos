## Backflow Prevention Opportunity (By-Law 068-2018)

This is the single strongest outreach angle.

**Key Facts:** [ALL VERIFIED from City of Thunder Bay website]
- **By-Law 068-2018** (Waterworks By-Law) mandates backflow prevention for ALL commercial, industrial, institutional, and multi-residential (5+ units) buildings
- In effect since **2008** — 18 years of compliance requirements
- **Annual testing required** — testable devices must be tested every year by a Certified Contractor
- **Cross-connection re-assessment every 5 years**
- **$50 annual renewal fee** added to water bill
- **Penalties: Up to $100,000 and/or water service termination** for non-compliance
