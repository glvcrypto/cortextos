## Personal Story

This is one of the most compelling generational stories in Northern Ontario construction.

**The Founder — Lanfranco "Frank" Faggioni (1929-2024)**
- Born in Morentella, province of Ancona, Italy
- Emigrated at age 18 "in search of a better life"
- Founded Faggioni and Sons Limited in 1962 (the website says 1956 — the earlier date likely reflects when Frank began working in construction, with incorporation in 1962)
- Built homes, apartment buildings, and renovations across Sudbury
- Known for "persistence, emphasis on quality and unwavering work ethic"
- Created the "leaky basement" specialization that became the company's core business
- Passed away September 26, 2024 at age 95
- Had two sons: Marcel and Peter
- Proudly passed the basement drainage business to nephew Fernando

**The Current Owner — Fern (Fernando) Faggioni**
- Nephew of Frank, carrying on the family legacy
- Inherited the specialized "Drain Wet Basement System" expertise
- Now runs the company as the second-generation operator
- The "F." in "F. Faggioni Construction" may originally refer to Frank, now conveniently also stands for Fern/Fernando

**Outreach significance:** The founder passed away just 18 months ago (Sept 2024). Fern is now the sole standard-bearer of a 70-year family legacy. This is a story that SELLS — Italian immigrant, post-war grit, 2,000+ basements, generational handoff. The marketing practically writes itself. But nobody is telling this story online.
