## Outreach Hooks

### HOOK 1: The Branded Traffic Trap
*"Your 210 monthly branded searches prove Thunder Bay knows your name after 33 years. But 73% of your website traffic comes from people who already know you. What about the 590 people searching 'plumbers thunder bay' every month who never see your name? You're on page 2 for that term."*

### HOOK 2: The Clow Darling Gap
*"Clow Darling ranks for 155 keywords and gets 1,401 visits/month. You rank for 62 keywords and get 989 visits. They've been around since 1937, but your showroom, spa brands, and renovation services give you content angles they can't match. You just need the SEO strategy to activate them."*

### HOOK 3: The Showroom Advantage
*"You're Thunder Bay's largest plumbing, spa, and pool showroom -- but someone searching 'hot tubs thunder bay' or 'bathroom renovations thunder bay' barely finds you. Your showroom is your competitive moat. Your website should be an extension of that showroom floor."*

### HOOK 4: The Zero-Content Problem
*"You carry Bullfrog, Viking, Freestyle, and Dreammaker spas. You do pool installations, water treatment, and bathroom renovations. But you have zero blog posts, zero buying guides, zero comparison content. Every one of those product lines is a content funnel waiting to drive traffic."*
