## Personal Story

**Founding Year:** March 2006 (operating); incorporated formally as "Dave's Heating and Cooling 2010 Limited" in 2010.

**Founding Story:** Dave Boissonneault started the business in 2006 out of Corbeil, a small community just outside North Bay proper. The business is explicitly family-owned and operated — Dave runs the field work and technical side; his wife Jeannie handles the administrative and contact functions. A third staff member, Terri-Lynn, appears on Alignable. Technicians Pat and Trent are referenced in a June 2024 Google review for installation work, suggesting the operation has grown beyond Dave alone.

**Community Ties:** The business sponsors a North Bay youth hockey team — "Dave's Heating U11 North Bay Trappers" appeared in GameSheetStats, a local league stats site. This is consistent with a locally-rooted family operator. The company was named Local Business Person of the Year 2022 on Alignable (community business network), reflecting strong local reputation.

**Service Philosophy:** The company's stated values — "honesty and integrity when dealing with, and providing services for our clients" — run through every listing and the website. Reviews consistently call out the no-pressure approach and quality of technicians. One reviewer noted Dave personally has been servicing their home since 2009, speaking to long-term customer relationships.

**Trade Identity:** Dave's is a Daikin Comfort Professional dealer (Daikin's premium dealer tier), offering 12-year parts and labour warranty on furnaces and A/C. They also carry Regency Fireplaces, Triangle Tube, IBC Technologies, Vermont Castings, Nordica, and Monessen — a broad hearth and HVAC product line suggesting they serve the full heating spectrum in a Northern Ontario climate.

## Niche-Marketing Misalignment

Dave's core acquisition model is word-of-mouth and directory presence. This worked for 20 years and built a 4.8-star reputation with nearly 340 Google reviews. But the HVAC buyer journey has shifted: emergency and high-intent searches ("furnace repair North Bay," "AC installation North Bay," "heat pump North Bay") all route through Google Search first. A thin website with no keyword content means Dave's is invisible for these searches.

Noll ClimateCare — founded 1984, 42 years, 13 staff, ClimateCare network — has a full content website, multiple service pages, a maintenance plan portal, and brand network support. When a new resident moves to North Bay and searches for an HVAC contractor, they find Noll. Dave's is found only if someone already knows the name.

The paradox: Dave's has more word-of-mouth equity and arguably better individual technician reviews than Noll, but Noll captures the top-of-funnel Google search traffic. Every 24/7 emergency call that Dave's can't capture because they don't rank is revenue going to a competitor with a smaller community reputation but a bigger digital footprint.
