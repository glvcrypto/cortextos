## Industry Context

- **74% of Canadian HVAC companies are micro-businesses** (5 or fewer employees). Industry is highly fragmented — no single company holds >5% national market share. (IBISWorld, 2025)
- **90% of HVAC customers search online before hiring.** Google Maps and reviews are the primary discovery channel. (NextLeft, 2025)
- **Heat pump sales in Canada grew 19% in 2025**, with 13% CAGR forecast through 2030, driven by Canada Greener Homes Grant (up to $10K rebates) and Ontario Home Renovations Savings Program ($7,500 available).
- **74% of homeowners expect HVAC service within 24 hours** when systems fail. Emergency calls are a primary driver of new customer acquisition.
- **42% of homeowners subscribe to maintenance plans.** Recurring revenue is a proven model and a strong SEO angle ("furnace tune-up Sudbury").

## GLV Ideal Client Profile (HVAC Sudbury)

The ideal GLV client in this niche is an established Sudbury HVAC company — typically 7–25 years in business — that has built a real operation (multiple trucks, licensed technicians, physical location) but has not invested in digital marketing. They likely rely on word of mouth, repeat clients, and maybe some print advertising. They have a website that was built years ago and hasn't been touched since, fewer than 200 Google reviews, and no content strategy. Their owner-operator makes decisions quickly and cares about results, not theories.

The pitch is simple: their competitors are getting calls they should be getting, because those competitors show up on Google and they don't. In Sudbury's HVAC market, even the top organic player only has 1,507 monthly visits — meaning the entire market is underserved digitally. A focused 6–12 month SEO campaign targeting the 7 zero-difficulty quick-win keywords alone could realistically deliver 30–50 new inbound sessions per month that didn't exist before. At a 5% conversion rate, that's 1–3 new leads per month from those terms alone — and a single furnace replacement at $5K–$9K covers months of retainer fees.

Climate Control Sudbury and Northern Climate are the highest-priority outreach targets: both have the establishment signals (years in business, dealer partnerships, financing) but are nearly invisible online relative to their potential. Kore HVAC and One Source are strong secondary targets for different angles (Chamber membership = business-minded; rural coverage = geographic SEO angle). Bouffard and KSM are longer-shot conversations but worth a call given their longevity and zero digital footprint.

**Avoid:** Campeau Heating (75 years, set in their ways), 669 Heat (already dominating, 2,175 reviews), national chains (Reliance, LG, Enercare — decisions made at HQ), and any solo-operator without capacity to handle increased lead volume.
