## Niche Pain Points

- **Most contractors have zero SEO presence.** The top organic results for "general contractor sudbury" are directory pages (Houzz, Sudbury.com, BBB) -- not actual contractor websites. This means the local pack / Google Maps is doing all the heavy lifting.
- **Branded search dominates.** The largest competitors (Pioneer, Belanger, Cast) get most of their traffic from people already searching their name. Almost nobody is ranking for high-intent service keywords.
- **Review counts are shockingly low.** Even the best-reviewed contractor (The General, 5.0 stars) has only 13 reviews. Most have under 10. In a market where 87% of homeowners avoid contractors below 4 stars, this is a massive vulnerability.
- **No content marketing exists.** None of the top 25 competitors appear to be running blogs, cost guides, or educational content. "How much does a bathroom renovation cost in Sudbury?" has zero local results.
- **Mobile-first gap.** With 70% of inquiries coming from mobile, many of these older contractor websites are poorly optimized for mobile UX, page speed, and click-to-call functionality.
